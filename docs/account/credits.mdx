# Credits

ACT3 AI uses a credit-based system to measure AI generation and rendering usage. Credits are consumed whenever you generate video, run renders, use AI tools, or export content. Every subscription plan includes a monthly credit allowance, and additional credits can be purchased at any time.

## How Credits Work

Credits are deducted in real time as you use the platform. The credit cost for any task depends on three factors:

- **Resolution** — Higher resolution costs more credits per second of output
- **Engine** — Premium AI engines (Google Veo 3.1, Grok Video) cost more than preview engines (Wan 2.1)
- **Duration** — Credits scale with the length of the generated clip or render

Before any generation or render job starts, ACT3 AI shows you the exact credit cost on the generate button — for example, **Generate [80 Credits]** or **Generate High Quality [400 Credits]**. If you do not have enough credits, a dialog tells you exactly how many you need and offers an upgrade path. You must confirm before credits are deducted.

## Credit Usage by Task

### Video Generation and Rendering

| Task | Draft / Preview | 1080p Final | 4K Final |
|---|---|---|---|
| Preview render (per 10s) | 1 credit | 2 credits | 4 credits |
| Full render (per 10s) | 3 credits | 5 credits | 10 credits |
| Google Veo 3.1 (per 10s) | — | 6 credits | 12 credits |
| Grok Video (per 10s) | — | 5 credits | 10 credits |
| Wan 2.1 session (per min) | 2 credits | 3 credits | — |

### Audio and Export

| Task | Credit Cost |
|---|---|
| TTS voice generation (per scene) | 1–2 credits |
| Subtitle generation | 1 credit per export |
| Multi-language audio dub | 3–5 credits per language |
| 4K export | Included in render cost |

## Monthly Credit Allowance by Plan

| Plan | Monthly Credits | Rollover Bank Cap |
|---|---|---|
| Free | 800 | 0 (no rollover) |
| Community | 8,000 | 1,000 |
| Standard | 33,000 | 100,000 |
| Business | 180,000 | 500,000 |
| Enterprise | Custom | Unlimited |

## Credit Rollover

Unused credits at the end of each billing period automatically roll into your Rollover Bank. Each plan has a maximum bank capacity as shown in the table above. Credits in the rollover bank are drawn from automatically when your monthly credits are exhausted. Rollover credits expire if you cancel or downgrade your subscription.

The Free plan has no rollover — unused credits expire at the end of each billing period.

## Buying Additional Credits

You can purchase credit top-ups at any time without changing your subscription:

1. Go to **Settings → Plans and Billing → Credit Store**
2. Choose a credit pack size
3. Credits are added to your balance immediately after purchase

Enterprise customers can negotiate custom credit rates and annual bundles through the sales team.

## Checking Your Credit Balance

Your current credit balance appears:

- In the top navigation bar on every page
- On the Dashboard overview panel
- In the Credits section of **Settings → Plans and Billing**

The Credits section also shows a breakdown of recent usage by project and by AI engine.

## Team Credit Pools

Organizations on Pro or Enterprise plans share a single credit pool across all team members. Owners and Admins can:

- View per-user and per-project credit usage reports
- Set credit limits for individual team members
- Purchase additional credits for the shared pool

Individual contributors draw from the shared pool automatically. Their usage is tracked per user for accountability.

## Managing Credits Efficiently

**Use Wan 2.1 for early-stage iteration** — Wan 2.1 costs 2 credits per minute at preview quality, which is significantly less than final-quality engines. Use it to test creative directions before committing to expensive renders.

**Use draft previews throughout production** — Draft renders cost 1–2 credits per 10 seconds compared to 5–10 for final quality. Validate your scene layout and timing in draft mode first.

**Lock completed shots** — Once a shot is approved, lock it in the Editor to prevent accidental regeneration that would consume credits unnecessarily.

**Batch-schedule renders overnight** — Queue all final renders in the Render Queue to run during off-hours. This ensures efficient use of credits without interrupting your editing workflow.

**Set billing alerts** — Configure alerts at 20% and 5% remaining credit balance from **Settings → Notifications** to avoid running out mid-production.

## Best Practices

- Test complex scenes at 720p or 1080p before committing to 4K renders
- Generate 3–5 Wan 2.1 preview variations to find the right creative direction before using Google Veo 3.1
- Plan your credit usage across a production before starting final renders
- Purchase extra credits before starting large production runs rather than waiting until your balance runs low
