# Billing

ACT3 AI uses a credit-based billing model with tiered subscription plans. Credits are consumed when generating AI video, images, and assets. Each plan includes monthly credits, cloud storage, and feature access.

## Subscription Plans

| Plan | Price / Month | Credits / Month | Rollover Cap | Concurrent Jobs | Watermark |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Free** | $0 | 800 | 0 | 1 | Yes |
| **Community** | $8 | 8,000 | 1,000 | 1 | No |
| **Standard** | $35 | 33,000 | 100,000 | 3 | No |
| **Business** | $175 | 180,000 | 500,000 | 6 | No |
| **Enterprise** | Contact Us | High volume | Unlimited | 10+ | No |

## Plan Details

### Free ($0/month)

- 800 credits per month, no rollover
- Personal use only; all generated videos include a watermark
- Available for the first 6 months of active use — after that, an upgrade to Standard or higher is required
- Assets are deleted after 4 months of inactivity
- Requires LinkedIn authentication

### Community ($8/month)

- 8,000 credits per month; 1,000 credit rollover cap
- No watermark on generated videos
- 1 concurrent render job
- Available for the first 6 months of active use
- Assets are deleted after 4 months of inactivity
- Requires LinkedIn authentication

### Standard ($35/month)

- 33,000 credits per month; 100,000 credit rollover cap
- No watermark; 3 concurrent render jobs
- Assets retained indefinitely while subscription is active
- No LinkedIn requirement

### Business ($175/month)

- 180,000 credits per month; 500,000 credit rollover cap
- No watermark; 6 concurrent render jobs
- **Commercial use rights** — required for distributing video commercially
- Watermarked dailies for IP provenance
- Private 2D and 3D sets
- Assets retained indefinitely while subscription is active

### Enterprise (Contact Us)

- 10M+ monthly credits; unlimited rollover
- 10+ concurrent render jobs; 4K video output
- Unlimited commercial use
- SAML SSO support
- Private 3D sets
- Priority support with SLA
- Dedicated account manager
- Custom pricing and contract terms

## The 6-Month Upgrade Requirement

Free and Community plans are trial and hobbyist tiers. After 6 months of active use, the platform requires an upgrade to Standard ($35/month) or higher. This ensures continued access to the platform for engaged users. You will receive notice before this requirement takes effect.

## Watermarks

All videos generated on the Free plan include an ACT3 AI watermark in the corner. Upgrading to Community ($8/month) or any higher plan removes the watermark from all generated content.

## Commercial Use

Commercial use rights — required when distributing generated video for revenue or business purposes — are included starting with the Business plan ($175/month). Free, Community, and Standard plans are for personal use. Check the Terms of Service for full licensing details.

## Intellectual Property and Watermarked Dailies

Your Organization owns all projects and generated content created within it. ACT3 AI maintains a rights ledger that timestamps every asset creation event. Watermarked dailies (timestamped intermediate renders that serve as proof of creation date) are available on Business and Enterprise plans and are useful for IP disputes, investor pitches, and festival submissions.

## Dormant Asset Deletion

Free and Community plan organizations have assets automatically deleted after 4 months of inactivity. Standard and higher plans retain assets indefinitely while the subscription is active. You receive an email warning before any deletion occurs.

## Monthly Spending Cap

In Organization Settings → Billing, you can enable a monthly spend cap and set a maximum dollar amount. When the cap is reached, all generation stops until the next billing cycle or you raise the cap. Individual member credit limits provide an additional layer of control.

## Credit Rollover

Unused credits roll over into a Rollover Bank at the end of each billing period. Each plan has a maximum bank capacity as shown in the plan table above. Credits in the rollover bank are drawn from automatically after your monthly credits are exhausted. Credits in the rollover bank expire if you cancel or downgrade your subscription.

## Changing Your Plan

Plans can be upgraded or downgraded at any time from **Settings → Plans and Billing**:

- **Upgrading** — Takes effect immediately. Unused credits from the current period transfer to your rollover bank.
- **Downgrading** — Takes effect at the end of the current billing period.
- **Cancellation** — Your account stays active until the end of the paid period. After that, access reverts to Free tier or is deactivated.

Visit [app.act3ai.com/settings/plans](https://app.act3ai.com/settings/plans) to view and manage your plan.

## Billing History

View invoices and payment receipts from **Settings → Billing → Billing History**. Each invoice includes:

- Billing period dates
- Plan tier and monthly charge
- Additional credit purchases
- Storage overages if applicable

## Payment Methods

ACT3 AI accepts:

- Major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- PayPal
- Enterprise customers: wire transfer and annual invoicing

Update your payment method at any time from **Settings → Billing → Payment Methods**.

## Best Practices

- Monitor your credit balance regularly from the Dashboard
- Set up billing alerts at 20% and 5% remaining balance
- Upgrade before large production runs to ensure sufficient credits
- Use draft renders throughout production to minimize credit consumption
