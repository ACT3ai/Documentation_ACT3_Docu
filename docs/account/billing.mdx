# Billing

ACT3 AI uses a credit-based billing model with tiered subscription plans. Credits are consumed when generating AI video, images, and assets. Each plan includes monthly credits, cloud storage, and feature access.

## Subscription Plans

| Plan | Price / Month | Credits / Month | Rollover Bank | Storage | Watermark |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Free** | $0 | 800 | None | 50 MB | Yes |
| **Starter** | $19 | 9,500 | 23,750 | 2 GB | Yes |
| **Creator** | $49 | 24,500 | 61,250 | 50 GB | No |
| **Pro** | $175 | 87,500 | 218,750 | 740 GB | No |
| **Enterprise** | Contact Us | High volume | Unlimited | Custom | No |

## Plan Details

### Free ($0/month)

- 800 credits per month, no rollover
- 50 MB cloud storage
- Personal use only; all videos include a watermark
- Available for the first 6 months only
- All content is always visible to the ACT3 AI community
- Includes: AI generation, camera controls, character casting and animation

### Starter ($19/month)

- 9,500 credits per month; 23,750 credit rollover bank
- 2 GB cloud storage
- Personal use; videos include watermark
- Available for the first 6 months only
- Includes: AI generation, camera controls, character casting and animation

### Creator ($49/month)

- 24,500 credits per month; 61,250 credit rollover bank
- 50 GB cloud storage
- Personal use; videos exported **without watermark**
- Generate 3 concurrent render jobs
- Includes: AI generation, camera controls, character casting and animation

### Pro ($175/month)

- 87,500 credits per month; 218,750 credit rollover bank
- 740 GB cloud storage
- Commercial use for audiences up to 100,000
- Generate 6 concurrent render jobs
- No watermark on videos
- Blender AI Story Controls and VFX Controls
- Create private 2D and 3D sets
- Includes: AI generation, camera controls, character casting and animation

### Enterprise (Contact Us)

- High monthly credit volume; custom rollover bank
- Custom cloud storage (4K video supported)
- Unlimited commercial use
- Generate 10+ concurrent render jobs
- No watermark on videos
- 4K video creation
- Priority support with SLA
- Blender AI Story, VFX, and Animation Controls
- Create private 2D and 3D sets
- Custom terms and negotiated pricing

## Credit Rollover

Unused credits roll over into a rollover bank at the end of each billing period. Your rollover bank has a capacity equal to 2.5x your monthly credit allowance. Credits in the rollover bank expire when you cancel or downgrade your subscription.

## Changing Your Plan

Plans can be upgraded or downgraded at any time from **Settings → Plans and Billing**:

- **Upgrading** — Takes effect immediately. Unused credits from the current period transfer to your rollover bank.
- **Downgrading** — Takes effect at the end of the current billing period.
- **Cancellation** — Your account stays active until the end of the paid period. After that, access reverts to Free tier or is deactivated.

Visit [app.act3ai.com/settings/plans](https://app.act3ai.com/settings/plans) to view and manage your plan.

## Billing History

View invoices and payment receipts from **Settings → Billing → Billing History**. Each invoice includes:

- Billing period dates
- Plan tier and monthly charge
- Additional credit purchases
- Storage overages if applicable

## Payment Methods

ACT3 AI accepts:

- Major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- PayPal
- Enterprise customers: wire transfer and annual invoicing

Update your payment method at any time from **Settings → Billing → Payment Methods**.

## Best Practices

- Monitor your credit balance regularly from the Dashboard
- Set up billing alerts at 20% and 5% remaining balance
- Upgrade before large production runs to ensure sufficient credits
- Use draft renders throughout production to minimize credit consumption
