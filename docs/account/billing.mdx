# Billing

ACT3 AI uses a credit-based billing model with tiered subscription plans. [Credits](/account/credits) are consumed when generating [AI video](/features/ai-video-generation), images, and assets. Each plan includes monthly credits, cloud storage, and feature access. For the full per-model cost catalog, see [Pricing Details](/account/pricing-details).

## Subscription Plans

| Plan | Price / Month | Credits / Month | Rollover Bank | Storage | Concurrent Jobs | Use Type |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Starter** | $49 | 245,000 | 612,500 (2.5 mo) | 50 GB | 3 | Personal |
| **Pro** | $175 | 875,000 | 2,187,500 (2.5 mo) | 740 GB | 6 | Commercial up to 100k audience |
| **Enterprise** | Contact Sales | Custom | 2.5 mo bank | Unlimited | 10+ | Unlimited commercial |

## Plan Details

### Starter ($49/month)

- 245,000 credits per month; rollover bank up to 612,500 credits (2.5 months)
- 50 GB cloud storage
- 3 concurrent render jobs
- **Personal use only** — for hobbyist and individual creative work
- Motion videos without watermark
- AI Image and Video Generation, Camera Controls, Character Casting and Animation
- Videos retained for 4 months after last activity

### Pro ($175/month)

- 875,000 credits per month; rollover bank up to 2,187,500 credits (2.5 months)
- 740 GB cloud storage
- 6 concurrent render jobs
- **Commercial use up to 100,000 audience** — suited for indie filmmakers, agencies, and growing creators
- Motion videos without watermark
- Power of Blender AI Story Controls and VFX Controls
- Create Private 2D and 3D Sets
- AI Image and Video Generation, Camera Controls, Character Casting and Animation

### Enterprise (Contact Sales)

- Custom monthly credit allowance, scaled to your production volume
- Unlimited cloud storage; 10+ concurrent render jobs
- **Unlimited commercial use**
- 4K video creation
- Priority support with SLA
- Power of Blender AI Story Controls and VFX Controls
- Create Private 2D and 3D Sets
- Motion videos without watermark
- Dedicated account manager and custom contract terms

## Commercial Use

Commercial use rights are included starting with the **Pro plan** ($175/month), which permits commercial distribution for audiences up to 100,000. **Enterprise** unlocks unlimited commercial use with no audience cap. The **Starter** plan is for personal, non-commercial use only. For team management, see [Teams](/account/teams). Check the Terms of Service for full licensing details.

## Intellectual Property

Your Organization owns all projects and generated content created within it. ACT3 AI maintains a rights ledger that timestamps every asset creation event — useful for IP disputes, investor pitches, and festival submissions.

## Dormant Asset Retention

Starter plan organizations have videos automatically deleted after 4 months of inactivity. Pro and Enterprise plans retain assets indefinitely while the subscription is active. You receive an email warning before any deletion occurs.

## Monthly Spending Cap

In [Organization Settings](/account/settings-organization) → Billing, you can enable a monthly spend cap and set a maximum dollar amount. When the cap is reached, all generation stops until the next billing cycle or you raise the cap. Individual member credit limits provide an additional layer of control.

## Credit Rollover

Unused credits roll over into a Rollover Bank at the end of each billing period. Each plan has a maximum bank capacity as shown in the plan table above. Credits in the rollover bank are drawn from automatically after your monthly credits are exhausted. Credits in the rollover bank expire if you cancel or downgrade your subscription.

## Changing Your Plan

Plans can be upgraded or downgraded at any time from **Settings → Plans and Billing**:

- **Upgrading** — Takes effect immediately. Unused credits from the current period transfer to your rollover bank.
- **Downgrading** — Takes effect at the end of the current billing period.
- **Cancellation** — Your account stays active until the end of the paid period. After that, the account is deactivated and remaining rollover credits expire.

Visit [app.act3ai.com/settings/plans](https://app.act3ai.com/settings/plans) to view and manage your plan.

## Billing History

View invoices and payment receipts from **Settings → Billing → Billing History**. Each invoice includes:

- Billing period dates
- Plan tier and monthly charge
- Additional credit purchases
- Storage overages if applicable

## Payment Methods

ACT3 AI accepts:

- Major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- PayPal
- Enterprise customers: wire transfer and annual invoicing

Update your payment method at any time from **Settings → Billing → Payment Methods**.

## Best Practices

- Monitor your credit balance regularly from the [Dashboard](/getting-started/dashboard)
- Set up billing alerts at 20% and 5% remaining balance
- Upgrade before large production runs to ensure sufficient credits
- Use draft renders throughout production to minimize credit consumption
