# Create Account

ACT3 AI accounts are created at [app.act3ai.com](https://app.act3ai.com). Registration is free and takes under two minutes. After signing up, you immediately receive free-tier credits and can start your first project.

## How to Sign Up

1. Go to [app.act3ai.com](https://app.act3ai.com)
2. Click **Sign Up**
3. Enter your full name, email address, and a password (minimum 8 characters)
4. Check the box to accept the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
5. Click **Create Account**
6. Open the confirmation email and click **Verify Email**
7. Log in with your credentials — you will land on the Dashboard

You can also sign up using a Google or Apple account by clicking the respective button on the sign-up page. No password is required when using social sign-in.

## Personal vs. Organization Accounts

**Personal Account** — For individual creators. One user, one billing plan, one credit balance. Best for independent filmmakers, social media creators, and anyone working alone.

**Organization Account** — For teams and studios. Multiple users share a workspace, asset library, and credit pool under a single subscription. Best for production teams, agencies, studios, and education groups.

You start with a personal account. You can create or join an organization at any time from the Dashboard. See [Teams](/docs/account/teams) for full details.

## What You Get at Sign-Up

When you create a new account:

- **800 free credits** on the Free plan to start generating right away
- **50 MB cloud storage** for projects and assets
- Access to AI generation, camera controls, character casting, and animation features
- Access to the AI Wizard for script-to-video guidance

The Free plan is available for the first 6 months. All Free-plan videos include a watermark. See [Billing](/docs/account/billing) for plan details and upgrade options.

## Managing Your Account

Once logged in, your account settings are at **Settings → Account** (click your profile icon in the top-right corner).

From Account Settings you can:

- Update your name, email address, and profile picture
- Change your password
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Manage active sessions and sign out of devices
- Connect or disconnect social sign-in (Google, Apple)
- Manage notification preferences

See [Account Settings](/docs/account/account-settings) for the full settings reference.

## Upgrading Your Plan

To unlock more credits, remove watermarks, and access advanced features, upgrade from **Settings → Plans and Billing**. You can upgrade at any time — changes take effect immediately.

See [Billing](/docs/account/billing) for a comparison of all available plans.

## Troubleshooting

**Didn't receive the confirmation email** — Check your spam or junk folder. If it's not there, use the **Resend Confirmation Email** link on the login page.

**Password reset not working** — Use the password reset form at [app.act3ai.com/password-reset](https://app.act3ai.com/password-reset). If the reset email doesn't arrive, check your spam folder or contact support.

**Social sign-in not working** — Make sure pop-ups are allowed in your browser. If you previously created an account with a different method using the same email, use that sign-in method instead.

**Account locked after too many login attempts** — Wait 15 minutes and try again, or use the password reset link.
