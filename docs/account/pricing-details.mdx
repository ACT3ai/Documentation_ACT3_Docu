# Pricing Details

This page shows the exact credit and dollar cost of every action you can run on ACT3 AI — every video model, every image model, audio, motion capture, render, and AI writing task. Use it to compare models side-by-side and to plan what you can produce on each [subscription plan](/account/billing).

For a higher-level overview, see [Billing](/account/billing) and [Credits](/account/credits).

## Credit Math

ACT3 AI uses a credit-based billing model. Every action shows you the credit cost up front before it runs.

| Conversion | Value |
|---|---|
| Default shot duration | 8 seconds |
| 4K image multiplier | 2× credits |

The exact in-app price is always shown on the generate button — for example, **Generate [2,080 Credits]** — before any credits are deducted.

### How a Shot Price Is Calculated

Every video model has a published **per-second rate** that depends on the model and the output resolution. Your shot price is simply:

> **Shot price = per-second rate × shot duration**

A shot defaults to 8 seconds. Higher resolutions process significantly more pixels per frame, so 1080p models cost more per second than 720p models — typically about 2.25× more, because a 1080p frame contains 2.25× the pixels of a 720p frame.

#### Worked Example — Seedance 2.0 at 1080p, 8 seconds

| Step | Value |
|---|---|
| Per-second rate at 1080p | $0.884 / second |
| Shot duration | × 8 seconds |
| **Shot price (USD)** | **$7.07** |
| **Shot price (credits)** | **3,536 credits** |

#### Compare with Seedance 2.0 Fast at 720p, 8 seconds

| Step | Value |
|---|---|
| Per-second rate at 720p | $0.314 / second |
| Shot duration | × 8 seconds |
| **Shot price (USD)** | **$2.51** |
| **Shot price (credits)** | **1,258 credits** |

That's why the 720p Fast variant of Seedance 2.0 is roughly **64% cheaper** for the same 8-second clip — same model family, lower resolution, less compute per frame.

| Assumption | Value |

| Story structure | 3 Acts → 12 Beats → 41 Scenes → 50 Shots |

### How to Spend Less on the Same Project

1. **Use draft / Fast models throughout iteration.** Stick to Seedance 2.0 Fast (720p) or Veo 3.1 Fast until you lock the cut. Switching only the hero shots to Seedance 2.0 (1080p) or Veo 3.1 standard cuts cost dramatically vs. running every shot through the premium model.
2. **Cut your shot count.** Story-structure costs scale per shot — going from 50 shots to 30 shots removes ~$80 of the total.
3. **Skip optional post-processing.** Auto-trim and Speech Replace are optional; you can omit them if you do not need automatic re-timing of dialogue.
4. **Approve and lock shots early.** Once a shot looks right, lock it so accidental re-generation does not consume credits twice.
