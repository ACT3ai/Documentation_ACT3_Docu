## Overview

The **Target Customers** of [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI") span across individual creators, production teams, and enterprises.
The platform is designed to serve a broad creative ecosystem — from YouTube influencers and TikTok creators to professional filmmakers, studios, and marketing agencies.

## Key Customer Segments

### 1. Independent Creators

- **Social Media Creators:** YouTubers, TikTokers, Instagram influencers.
- **Indie Filmmakers:** Students, freelancers, and hobbyists experimenting with storytelling.
- **Content Educators:** Teachers and trainers creating explainer or instructional videos.
- Related Pages: [Social Media Creators](/docs/legacy-wiki/social-media-creators "Social Media Creators"), [Social Media Videos](/docs/legacy-wiki/social-media-videos "Social Media Videos"), [Marketing Videos](/docs/legacy-wiki/marketing-videos "Marketing Videos")

### 2. Professional Filmmakers & Studios

- Writers, directors, and producers testing story concepts before full production.
- Previsualization of scripts using [Set Design](/docs/legacy-wiki/set-design "Set Design"), [Digital Actors](/docs/legacy-wiki/digital-actors "Digital Actors"), and [Storyboards](/docs/legacy-wiki/storyboard-panels "Storyboard Panels").
- Low-cost concept films, trailers, and visual lookbooks.
- Related Pages: [Build Video](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video"), [Rendering System](/docs/legacy-wiki/rendering-system "Rendering System"), [Export Distribution](/docs/legacy-wiki/export-distribution "Export Distribution")

### 3. Marketing Teams & Agencies

- In-house creative teams producing branded content.
- Ad agencies building campaigns for clients at scale.
- Product launch explainers, demo reels, and [marketing campaigns](/docs/legacy-wiki/marketing-videos "Marketing Videos").
- Related Pages: [Marketing Videos](/docs/legacy-wiki/marketing-videos "Marketing Videos"), [Style Presets](/docs/legacy-wiki/style-presets "Style Presets"), [Export Distribution](/docs/legacy-wiki/export-distribution "Export Distribution")

### 4. Animation & VFX Studios

- Concept prototyping for animated series or films.
- Integrating AI-rendered sets with USD/FBX workflows.
- Motion capture and [Digital Actor](/docs/legacy-wiki/digital-actors "Digital Actors") integration.
- Related Pages: [Import B-Roll](/docs/legacy-wiki/import-broll "Import BRoll"), [File Formats](/docs/legacy-wiki/file-formats "File Formats"), [Set Design](/docs/legacy-wiki/set-design "Set Design")

### 5. Enterprise & Corporate Customers

- Corporate training videos, onboarding, and internal communications.
- Investor pitch decks with auto-generated pitch decks and video explainers.
- Related Pages: [Organization Management](/docs/legacy-wiki/organization-management "Organization Management"), [Subscription Plan](/docs/legacy-wiki/subscription-plan "Subscription Plan"), [Billing Model](/docs/legacy-wiki/billing-model "Billing Model")

### 6. Educational Institutions

- Universities, film schools, and workshops using ACT 3 AI for learning.
- Student filmmakers experimenting with low-cost AI-driven production.
- Research projects in generative video and storytelling.
- Related Pages: [Documentation Hub](/docs/legacy-wiki/documentation "Documentation"), [Generative Workflow Pipeline](/docs/legacy-wiki/generative-workflow-pipeline "Generative Workflow Pipeline")

## Value Proposition by Segment

- **Creators:** Access to studio-grade production at low cost.
- **Studios:** Rapid prototyping and visual testing.
- **Marketers:** Fast campaign turnarounds with brand consistency.
- **Enterprises:** Scalable video communication with localization support.
- **Education:** Affordable training tools for next-generation filmmakers.
