## Overview

The **Organization** feature in [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI") allows you to manage multiple team members, share resources, and collaborate on [projects](/docs/legacy-wiki/new-project "New Project") under a single workspace.
It’s ideal for production teams, studios, agencies, and educational groups who need shared access to scripts, [digital actors](/docs/legacy-wiki/digital-actors "Digital Actors"), assets, and [render queues](/docs/legacy-wiki/render-queue "Render Queue").

## Key Benefits

- Centralized project storage for all members
- Role-based permissions for secure collaboration
- Shared [credit pool](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System") for rendering and AI generation
- Access to [billing](/docs/legacy-wiki/billing-model "Billing Model") and [subscription management](/docs/legacy-wiki/subscription-plan "Subscription Plan") in one place

## Organization Structure

An Organization in ACT 3 AI is structured into:

- **Organization Owner** – Full permissions, billing control
- **Admins** – Manage members, assign roles, edit all content
- **Editors** – Create and edit [scenes](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes"), [shots](/docs/legacy-wiki/shots "Shots"), and scripts
- **Viewers** – Read-only access to projects and assets

## How to Create an Organization

1. Go to your [Dashboard](/docs/legacy-wiki/dashboard "Dashboard").
2. Click **Create Organization** in the sidebar.
3. Enter an organization name and optional description.
4. Choose your subscription plan and payment method.
5. Invite initial members by email.

## Inviting and Managing Members

- Invite members via email or by sharing a secure invite link.
- Assign roles upon invitation or later in the Organization Settings panel.
- Remove or reassign members as team needs change.

## Permissions Overview

| Role | Project Access | Credit Usage | Billing Access |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Owner | Full control | Yes | Yes |
| Admin | Full control | Yes | No |
| Editor | Edit assigned projects | Yes | No |
| Viewer | View-only | No | No |

## Shared Asset Library

All members can access:

- [Digital Actors](/docs/legacy-wiki/digital-actors "Digital Actors")
- [Storyboards](/docs/legacy-wiki/storyboard-panels "Storyboard Panels")
- [Top-Down Layouts](/docs/legacy-wiki/top-down-view "Top Down View")
- [Imported Scripts](/docs/legacy-wiki/import-script "Import Script")

## Credit Management

- Credits are pooled at the organization level.
- Owners/Admins can allocate credits to specific members.
- Credit usage logs are available under [Credit Reports](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System").

## Collaboration Features

- Real-time co-editing in [Editor Workspace](/docs/legacy-wiki/editor "Editor").
- Inline commenting system for feedback.
- Locking [scenes](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes") or [shots](/docs/legacy-wiki/shots "Shots") to prevent accidental changes.

## Best Practices

- Set clear roles for every member to maintain workflow security.
- Review credit usage regularly to avoid over-consumption.
- Use folders to organize projects by client or production type.

## Troubleshooting

- **Members not receiving invites** → Check spam/junk folders and resend.
- **Cannot edit project** → Confirm role permissions in Organization Settings.
- **Credits not showing** → Ensure the member is part of the correct organization.
