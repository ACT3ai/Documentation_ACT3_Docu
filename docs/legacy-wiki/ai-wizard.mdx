## Overview

The **AI Wizard** in [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI") is your intelligent assistant for generating story structures, beats, and creative prompts.
It guides you through transforming a rough concept into a structured script, complete with [story arcs](/docs/legacy-wiki/story-arcs "Story Arcs"), [scenes](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes"), and [shot-level prompts](/docs/legacy-wiki/shot-prompting "Shot Prompting").
Designed for both beginners and professionals, AI Wizard accelerates pre-production while leaving room for full creative control.

## Key Capabilities

- **Idea Expansion:** Turn a one-sentence concept into a multi-act outline.
- **Story Arc Generation:** Automatically propose [Acts](/docs/legacy-wiki/acts "Acts"), [scenes](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes"), and [beats](/docs/legacy-wiki/story-beats "Story Beats") with pacing suggestions.
- **Character Suggestions:** Recommend [Characters](/docs/legacy-wiki/characters "Characters") and [Digital Actors](/docs/legacy-wiki/digital-actors "Digital Actors") aligned with tone and genre.
- **Visual Prompts:** Generate first-pass [camera directions](/docs/legacy-wiki/camera-movement "Camera Movement"), [lighting](/docs/legacy-wiki/lighting "Lighting") ideas, and [B-Roll](/docs/legacy-wiki/b-roll "B-roll") suggestions.
- **Interactive Refinement:** Adjust plot points or regenerate sections with one click.

## How to Use

1. From the Dashboard, click **New Project → AI Wizard**.
2. Enter your initial idea or upload a short [imported script](/docs/legacy-wiki/import-script "Import Script").
3. Select genre, mood, and format (film, ad, TikTok, trailer).
4. Review the generated outline in [Timeline & Storyboard](/docs/legacy-wiki/timeline-and-storyboard "Timeline & Storyboard").
5. Approve or refine; the wizard saves directly into your [Editor](/docs/legacy-wiki/editor "Editor").

## Pricing & Credits

- Using AI Wizard consumes [credits](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System") based on the scope of generation:
  - Short outline (1–2 minutes): minimal credits.
  - Full script with multiple acts: higher credit usage.
- Credit costs are displayed in real time before generation.

## Troubleshooting & Tips

- For best results, describe the tone (e.g., “dark comedy,” “epic sci-fi”) when starting.
- Keep inputs concise; long descriptions can reduce focus.
- Use inline feedback to regenerate specific acts instead of redoing the whole script.
- Combine with [Google Veo 3](/docs/legacy-wiki/google-veo-3 "Google Veo 3") or [Runway Integration](/docs/legacy-wiki/runway "Runway") for rapid pre-visualization.
