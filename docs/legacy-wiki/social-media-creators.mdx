## Overview

**Social Media Creators** use [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI") to rapidly generate short-form and long-form video content for platforms such as YouTube, TikTok, Instagram Reels, and Facebook.
The platform streamlines every stage — from idea generation and [story structure](/docs/legacy-wiki/ai-wizard "AI Wizard") to [final editing](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video") and [exporting](/docs/legacy-wiki/export-distribution "Export Distribution") in platform-optimized formats.

## Use Cases

- **YouTube Creators** – Vlogs, explainers, animations, cinematic shorts.
- **TikTok Creators** – 15–60 second vertical videos, fast-paced edits, trends.
- **Instagram Creators** – 9:16 Reels or 1:1 square promos.
- **Marketing Influencers** – Sponsored content, product showcases, and reviews.
- **Education/Explainers** – Short instructional videos with clear messaging.

## Workflow for Social Media Creators

### Step 1: Start a Project

- Select **[New Project](/docs/legacy-wiki/new-project "New Project")** → choose “Social Media” as project type.
- Choose aspect ratio:
  - 9:16 – TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts
  - 16:9 – YouTube long-form
  - 1:1 – Instagram/Facebook square ads
- Keep duration short (15–90 seconds) for maximum engagement.

### Step 2: Generate Story & Script

- Use [AI Wizard](/docs/legacy-wiki/ai-wizard "AI Wizard") to create a snappy logline and beats.
- Draft your script in the [Script Editor](/docs/legacy-wiki/script-editor "Script Editor") or [import existing scripts](/docs/legacy-wiki/import-script "Import Script").
- Focus on hooks in the first 3–5 seconds.

### Step 3: Visuals & Actors

- Cast [Digital Actors](/docs/legacy-wiki/digital-actors "Digital Actors") or add [characters](/docs/legacy-wiki/characters "Characters") tailored to your audience.
- Apply branded styling, wardrobes, or logos.
- Plan [storyboards](/docs/legacy-wiki/storyboard-panels "Storyboard Panels") and [camera setups](/docs/legacy-wiki/top-down-view "Top Down View") for dynamic visuals.

### Step 4: Rendering

- Use [WAN AI Integration](/docs/legacy-wiki/wan "Wan") for fast previews.
- Render final sequences with [Google Veo 3](/docs/legacy-wiki/google-veo-3 "Google Veo 3") (realistic) or [Runway Integration](/docs/legacy-wiki/runway "Runway") (stylized).
- Optimize resolution (1080p or 720p) for faster social media publishing.

### Step 5: Export & Publish

- Export MP4 via [Export Distribution](/docs/legacy-wiki/export-distribution "Export Distribution") for direct upload.
- Auto-generate subtitles for accessibility and reach.
- Create multi-language dubs for global distribution.
- Upload directly to YouTube, TikTok, or Instagram.

## Best Practices

- **Hook First:** Grab attention in the opening 3 seconds.
- **Keep It Short:** Under 30s for TikTok/Reels; 2–5 min for YouTube.
- **Vertical Format:** Use 9:16 for maximum visibility on mobile.
- **A/B Testing:** Export variations with different [actors](/docs/legacy-wiki/characters "Characters"), CTAs, or music.
- **Engagement Boosters:** Add captions, emojis, and bold overlays.

## Example Project Types

- TikTok skits and short comedy sketches.
- YouTube tutorials or cinematic shorts.
- Instagram lifestyle or product reels.
- Branded influencer campaigns.

## Troubleshooting

- **Export blurry in TikTok** → Ensure 1080p and vertical aspect ratio selected.
- **Audience retention low** → Shorten script, add stronger hook.
- **Video rejected by platform** → Check platform ad/policy guidelines.
- **File too large** → Export in 1080p instead of 4K.
