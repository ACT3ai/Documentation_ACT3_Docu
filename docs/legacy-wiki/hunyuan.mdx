# Hunyuan Integration

## Overview

The **Hunyuan Integration** in [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI") provides access to Tencent’s advanced multimodal AI model for video, image, and creative asset generation.
Hunyuan is designed for high-fidelity scene rendering, character realism, and cultural localization—making it especially useful for [film production](/docs/legacy-wiki/film-and-tv-professionals "Film and TV Professionals"), [animation](/docs/legacy-wiki/animation-studios "Animation Studios"), and region-specific [marketing content](/docs/legacy-wiki/marketing-agencies "Marketing Agencies").

## Key Capabilities

- **Text-to-Video Rendering:** Generate cinematic sequences directly from written descriptions.
- **Cultural Style Adaptation:** Apply localized visual aesthetics, traditional art styles, or regional cinematography techniques.
- **High-Resolution Output:** Supports up to 4K renders for final production use.
- **Advanced Face & Motion Capture:** Produces natural character motion and facial expressions when paired with [Digital Actors](/docs/legacy-wiki/digital-actors "Digital Actors").
- **Asset Generation:** Create props, backdrops, and textures for [set design](/docs/legacy-wiki/set-design "Set Design") workflows.

## How to Use

1. From the [Editor workspace](/docs/legacy-wiki/editor "Editor"), select a [scene](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes") or [shot](/docs/legacy-wiki/shots "Shots") to render with Hunyuan.
2. Click **AI → Hunyuan** to open the integration panel.
3. Enter your prompt, including camera details, scene mood, and cultural or stylistic notes.
4. Choose desired resolution, frame rate, and aspect ratio.
5. Click **Generate** and preview results in the Preview|Preview panel.
6. Accept or regenerate until satisfied, then add to the [timeline](/docs/legacy-wiki/timeline-and-storyboard "Timeline & Storyboard") or [render queue](/docs/legacy-wiki/render-queue "Render Queue").

## Pricing & Credits

- Hunyuan tasks consume credits from the [Credit System](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System") based on resolution and render length.
- Example: 4K render at 10 seconds may cost 10 credits; 1080p preview at 10 seconds may cost 4 credits.

## Tips & Best Practices

- Use Hunyuan for culturally specific environments and costumes that other models may not handle as well.
- Combine Hunyuan outputs with [Comfy UI](/docs/legacy-wiki/comfy-ui "Comfy UI") pipelines for advanced compositing.
- For large projects, use [WAN AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/wan "Wan") for previews before committing full-quality Hunyuan renders.

## Common Use Cases

- Historical or culturally themed film sequences.
- Realistic animated character performances.
- High-quality [set renders](/docs/legacy-wiki/set-design "Set Design") for pitch decks or previsualization.
- Region-specific advertising campaigns.
