## Overview

**B-Roll** in [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI") refers to supplemental footage that supports the main narrative.
It provides atmospheric shots, cutaway visuals, or contextual background imagery that makes your video feel more polished and cinematic.
Within the ACT 3 AI workflow, B-Roll can be imported, AI-generated, or mixed with primary scene footage for maximum flexibility.

## Why Use B-Roll?

- Adds context and visual richness between primary shots.
- Smooths over transitions in [timelines](/docs/legacy-wiki/editor "Editor") or [scenes](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes").
- Enhances professional storytelling for ads, short films, or marketing videos.
- Provides additional camera coverage without reshooting core scenes.

## Importing B-Roll

1. From the sidebar, choose **[Import B-Roll](/docs/legacy-wiki/import-broll "Import BRoll")**.
2. Upload your own clips or select from stock footage libraries.
3. Tag imported footage with scene, location, or mood metadata.
4. Place B-Roll on a separate track in the [Timeline View](/docs/legacy-wiki/timeline-view "Timeline View") for flexible editing.

## Generating B-Roll with AI

- Use [Google Veo 3](/docs/legacy-wiki/google-veo-3 "Google Veo 3") or [Runway Integration](/docs/legacy-wiki/runway "Runway") for atmospheric cutaways (e.g., cityscapes, crowd shots, establishing landscapes).
- Combine with [WAN AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/wan "Wan") for rapid, low-latency previews during collaborative sessions.
- Adjust [shot prompts](/docs/legacy-wiki/shot-prompting "Shot Prompting") for mood-specific transitions (e.g., “slow pan across neon-lit city at night”).

## Best Practices

- Keep clips between 3–10 seconds to maintain pacing.
- Use B-Roll to establish mood or break up long dialogues.
- Mix imported stock with AI-generated footage for unique visuals.
- Add [credit-efficient previews](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System") before committing to final renders.

## Example Use Cases

- Marketing ad: Cutaway to audience reactions.
- Short film: Establishing exterior shot between dialogue-heavy scenes.
- TikTok: Quick transitions between primary content and context visuals.
