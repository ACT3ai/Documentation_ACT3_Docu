# Runway Model Integration

## Overview

The **Runway Model Integration** in [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI") allows creators to generate, refine, and stylize AI-driven video clips directly from text prompts or storyboard instructions.
Runway’s generative AI specializes in cinematic styles, fast prototyping, and creative experimentation, making it a flexible option alongside [Google Veo 3](/docs/legacy-wiki/google-veo-3 "Google Veo 3") and [WAN AI Integration](/docs/legacy-wiki/wan "Wan").

## Key Capabilities

- **Prompt-to-Video:** Generate high-quality video from text descriptions or scene inputs.
- **Style Transfer:** Apply cinematic looks, filters, and moods (e.g., noir, documentary, anime).
- **Frame Interpolation:** Smooth transitions between shots or extend short clips.
- **Multi-Modal Input:** Combine text prompts with images, audio, or motion cues for richer results.
- **Quick Iterations:** Rapidly generate variations for experimentation during pre-production.

## How to Use

1. Open the [Editor workspace](/docs/legacy-wiki/editor "Editor") and select a shot or sequence.
2. Click **AI → Runway Model**.
3. Enter a descriptive [shot prompt](/docs/legacy-wiki/shot-prompting "Shot Prompting") or select storyboard assets.
4. Configure output: resolution, frame rate, duration, and style preset.
5. Click **Generate**: the Runway system creates a clip, which appears in your [Preview panel](/docs/legacy-wiki/preview "Preview").
6. Accept the output, regenerate variations, or push the clip to the [Timeline View](/docs/legacy-wiki/timeline "Timeline").

## Pricing & Credits

- Runway tasks consume [credits](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System") depending on clip length and resolution.
- Preview renders are lower-cost, while full exports (1080p or higher) use more credits.

## Troubleshooting & Tips

- **Be concise:** Short, vivid prompts work best for Runway’s model.
- **Experiment with styles:** Use cinematic presets to quickly test creative directions.
- **Combine with other models:** Use [Google Veo 3](/docs/legacy-wiki/google-veo-3 "Google Veo 3") for realism and Runway for stylized looks.
- **Refine with Editor:** Place Runway-generated clips into the [Timeline](/docs/legacy-wiki/timeline "Timeline") and sync with [Digital Actors](/docs/legacy-wiki/digital-actors "Digital Actors") or [Camera Instructions](/docs/legacy-wiki/cameras "Cameras").
