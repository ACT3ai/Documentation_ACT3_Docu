## Overview

- - In-House Marketing Teams** can use [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI") to rapidly produce **high-quality video content** for campaigns, social channels, internal communications, and brand storytelling — without relying heavily on external production agencies.

By leveraging ACT 3 AI’s **AI-powered writing, directing, cinematography, and rendering tools**, marketing teams can respond quickly to trends, adapt campaigns in real time, and reduce the cost and complexity of professional video creation.

## Benefits for Marketing Departments

- **Speed to Market:** Launch videos within hours instead of weeks.
- **Brand Consistency:** Maintain consistent tone, style, and messaging across multiple campaigns.
- **Cost Efficiency:** Reduce dependency on expensive shoots, crews, and post-production.
- **Creative Agility:** Rapidly iterate on concepts based on real-time campaign performance data.

## Key Features for Marketing Teams

- **[New Project](/docs/legacy-wiki/new-project "New Project")** – Start branded video projects quickly with predefined templates.
- **[AI Writing](/docs/legacy-wiki/ai-writing "AI Writing")** – Generate on-brand scripts from a simple brief or product description.
- **[AI Directing](/docs/legacy-wiki/ai-directing "AI Directing")** – Ensure visual tone aligns with brand guidelines.
- **[Cinematography](/docs/legacy-wiki/cinematography "Cinematography")** – Customize camera movements, lighting, and framing for a signature brand look.
- **[Set Design](/docs/legacy-wiki/set-design "Set Design")** – Create virtual environments that match product or lifestyle themes.
- **[Digital Actors](/docs/legacy-wiki/digital-actors "Digital Actors")** – Represent diverse customer demographics or use virtual brand ambassadors.
- **[Build Video](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video")** – Render ready-to-publish ads, explainers, or event promos.

## Example Use Cases

- **Product Launch Videos:** Show product features with AI-generated visuals and voiceovers.
- **Social Media Campaigns:** Create multiple video variations for A/B testing across platforms.
- **Internal Communication:** Produce CEO messages, training content, or milestone updates.
- **Event Promotion:** Generate teaser trailers and highlight reels for corporate events.

## Workflow for Marketing Teams

1. Define campaign objectives and messaging.
2. Start a [New Project](/docs/legacy-wiki/new-project "New Project") with campaign-specific templates.
3. Use [AI Writing](/docs/legacy-wiki/ai-writing "AI Writing") to draft scripts that fit the campaign tone.
4. Design scenes in [Storyboard Panels](/docs/legacy-wiki/storyboard-panels "Storyboard Panels") with matching visuals.
5. Render previews, collect team feedback, and finalize in [Build Video](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video").
6. Export in formats optimized for [target platforms](/docs/legacy-wiki/file-formats "File Formats") (e.g., 16:9 for YouTube, 9:16 for TikTok).
