## Overview

The **Creating Movies** workflow in [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI") takes you from an idea or script to a fully rendered, AI-generated video project.
Whether you are making a short film, ad, social clip, or full-length feature, ACT 3 AI provides a guided path with tools for [writing](/docs/legacy-wiki/script-editor "Script Editor"), [structuring](/docs/legacy-wiki/ai-wizard "AI Wizard"), [casting](/docs/legacy-wiki/digital-actors "Digital Actors"), and [assembly](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video").

## Steps to Create a Movie

### Step 1: Start a Project

- Go to [New Project](/docs/legacy-wiki/new-project "New Project") in the dashboard.
- Enter a title, description, and choose project type (Film, YouTube, TikTok, Commercial).
- Select aspect ratio (16:9, 9:16, 1:1) and duration.

### Step 2: Shape the Story

- Use the [AI Wizard](/docs/legacy-wiki/ai-wizard "AI Wizard") to generate logline, acts, and beats.
- Import or write your script in the [Script Editor](/docs/legacy-wiki/script-editor "Script Editor").
- Auto-format scene headings, character names, and dialogue.

### Step 3: Build Characters & Cast

- Define [characters](/docs/legacy-wiki/characters "Characters") (name, role, backstory).
- Assign [digital actors](/docs/legacy-wiki/digital-actors "Digital Actors") with custom wardrobe, style, and voices.
- Use [motion capture](/docs/legacy-wiki/motion-capture "Motion Capture") for realistic movement if needed.

### Step 4: Visual Planning

- Block action in [Top-Down View](/docs/legacy-wiki/top-down-view "Top Down View") (cameras, lights, props).
- Use [Storyboard & Panels](/docs/legacy-wiki/storyboard-panels "Storyboard Panels") for shot sketches or imported art.
- Sequence events in the [timeline editor](/docs/legacy-wiki/timeline "Timeline").

### Step 5: Generate Video Clips

- Select scenes or shots and render with:
  - [Google Veo 3 Integration](/docs/legacy-wiki/google-veo-3 "Google Veo 3") – photorealism
  - [Runway Integration](/docs/legacy-wiki/runway "Runway") – stylized & experimental
  - [WAN AI Integration](/docs/legacy-wiki/wan "Wan") – low-latency collaboration previews
- Adjust resolution (Draft → 4K) and preview costs in the [Credit System](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System") panel.

### Step 6: Import Media

- Add external media via [Import Script](/docs/legacy-wiki/import-script "Import Script") or [Import B-Roll](/docs/legacy-wiki/import-broll "Import BRoll").
- Sync voiceovers, audio tracks, or external assets.

### Step 7: Assemble & Edit

- Use [Build Video](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video") to combine shots, transitions, and sound.
- Apply [color grading](/docs/legacy-wiki/color-grading "Color Grading") and style adjustments.
- Lock scenes once approved.

### Step 8: Render & Export

- Send jobs to the [Render Queue](/docs/legacy-wiki/render-queue "Render Queue") for overnight or batch rendering.
- Export in MP4, MOV, or ProRes at 1080p or 4K.
- Deliver pitch decks, lookbooks, and thumbnails automatically.
- Export scripts via [Export Script](/docs/legacy-wiki/export-script "Export Script") if needed.

## Tips for Success

- **Start small:** Use [draft previews](/docs/legacy-wiki/draft-previews "Draft Previews") to experiment cheaply before final 4K renders.
- **Be specific:** More detailed prompts yield better AI outputs.
- **Iterate quickly:** Use [WAN AI Integration](/docs/legacy-wiki/wan "Wan") for fast creative collaboration.
- **Match style:** Keep consistent lighting and LUTs across all shots.
- **Lock versions:** Use scene locking in [Timeline](/docs/legacy-wiki/timeline "Timeline") for stability.

## Troubleshooting

- Scenes not rendering → Check [credit balance](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System").
- Inconsistent characters → Ensure [character profiles](/docs/legacy-wiki/characters "Characters") are linked to [Digital Actors](/docs/legacy-wiki/digital-actors "Digital Actors").
- Long renders → Queue in [Render Queue](/docs/legacy-wiki/render-queue "Render Queue") during off-peak hours.
