## Overview

The **Organization Management** system in [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI") enables teams, studios, and enterprises to collaborate on shared projects.
It provides role-based access, shared [credit pools](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System"), billing centralization, and real-time collaboration in the [Editor workspace](/docs/legacy-wiki/editor "Editor").

## Key Features

- **Shared Credit Pool:** Centralized [credits](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System") for the entire team.
- **Role-Based Access:** Define admins, editors, reviewers, and viewers.
- **Project Collaboration:** Multiple team members can co-edit [scripts](/docs/legacy-wiki/scripts "Scripts"), [scenes](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes"), and [video projects](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video").
- **Billing Consolidation:** Single invoice and [billing plan](/docs/legacy-wiki/billing-model "Billing Model") for the organization.
- **Security & Permissions:** Assign access at project or folder level.

## Roles

- **Owner:** Sets up the organization, manages billing, assigns roles.
- **Admin:** Manages users, roles, and [subscription plans](/docs/legacy-wiki/subscription-plan "Subscription Plan").
- **Editor:** Full creative access to [Editor](/docs/legacy-wiki/editor "Editor"), [AI Wizard](/docs/legacy-wiki/ai-wizard "AI Wizard"), and [Build Video](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video").
- **Reviewer:** Can view projects and leave comments.
- **Viewer:** Read-only access for stakeholders.

## How to Set Up an Organization

1. Go to **Account Settings → Organization Management**.
2. Click **Create Organization**.
3. Enter organization name, description, and billing details.
4. Invite team members via email or link.
5. Assign roles and configure credit sharing.

## Collaboration Workflow

- Multiple users can edit [scripts](/docs/legacy-wiki/script-editor "Script Editor") or [timelines](/docs/legacy-wiki/timeline "Timeline") simultaneously.
- Lock scenes once approved to prevent overwrites.
- Leave inline [comments](/docs/legacy-wiki/collaboration "Collaboration") for feedback.
- Track changes in the [version history log](/docs/legacy-wiki/version-history "Version History").

## Billing & Credits

- Shared billing handled under one [plan](/docs/legacy-wiki/billing-model "Billing Model").
- [Subscription tiers](/docs/legacy-wiki/subscription-plan "Subscription Plan") define how many credits the team receives monthly.
- Owners and admins can purchase additional credits.
- Credit usage is tracked per user and per project for accountability.

## Best Practices

- Use separate project folders for campaigns, clients, or departments.
- Assign roles carefully — avoid giving editor rights to reviewers.
- Lock finalized projects to protect master versions.
- Monitor credit usage regularly in [Credit Dashboard](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System").

## Troubleshooting

- **Members not seeing credits** → Ensure they are part of the shared credit pool.
- **Projects missing** → Check permissions and role assignments.
- **Billing issues** → Review the billing history page or contact support.
