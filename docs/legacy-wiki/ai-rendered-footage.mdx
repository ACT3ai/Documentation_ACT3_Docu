## Overview

The **AI Rendered Footage** workflow in [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI") allows creators to transform their scripts, scenes, and storyboards into high-quality rendered video clips using AI-driven rendering engines.
This process combines your [script editing](/docs/legacy-wiki/script-editing "Script Editing"), [storyboards](/docs/legacy-wiki/storyboard-panels "Storyboard Panels"), and [camera instructions](/docs/legacy-wiki/camera-instructions "Camera Instructions") into polished video output.

## Key Capabilities

- **Cinematic Rendering:** Generate film-like shots using advanced AI rendering.
- **Multi-Engine Support:** Export to services like [Google Veo 3](/docs/legacy-wiki/google-veo-3 "Google Veo 3"), [Runway Integration](/docs/legacy-wiki/runway "Runway"), or [WAN AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/wan "Wan").
- **Asset Integration:** Incorporate [digital actors](/docs/legacy-wiki/digital-actors "Digital Actors"), [set designs](/docs/legacy-wiki/set-design "Set Design"), and [media assets](/docs/legacy-wiki/media-library "Media Library") directly into renders.
- **Flexible Outputs:** Export clips in different resolutions, aspect ratios, and formats depending on your [subscription plan](/docs/legacy-wiki/subscription-plan "Subscription Plan").

## How to Use

1. Finalize your [scenes](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes") and [shots](/docs/legacy-wiki/shots "Shots") inside the [Editor workspace](/docs/legacy-wiki/editor "Editor").
2. Add any [music sync](/docs/legacy-wiki/music-sync "Music Sync"), [B-Roll](/docs/legacy-wiki/b-roll "B-roll").
3. Go to **Build → Render Video** in the top menu.
4. Select the rendering engine (e.g., [Google Veo 3](/docs/legacy-wiki/google-veo-3 "Google Veo 3"), [Runway](/docs/legacy-wiki/runway "Runway"), or [WAN AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/wan "Wan")).
5. Adjust output settings (resolution, bitrate, format).
6. Start the render; progress will display in the [Render Queue](/docs/legacy-wiki/render-queue "Render Queue").

## Best Practices

- Use [structured story arcs](/docs/legacy-wiki/story-structure "Story Structure") before rendering for smoother narrative flow.
- Choose WAN AI for previews, then finalize with Google Veo 3 or Runway for maximum quality.
- Always review [credit consumption](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System") before starting long renders.

## Troubleshooting

- If renders are low-quality, check your [camera setup](/docs/legacy-wiki/camera-angles "Camera Angles") and lighting design.
- For slow processing, switch to [WAN AI Previews](/docs/legacy-wiki/wan "Wan") to iterate faster.
- If credits run out mid-render, you can purchase [extra credits](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System") from your [plan page](/docs/legacy-wiki/subscription-plan "Subscription Plan").
