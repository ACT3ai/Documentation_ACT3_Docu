## Overview

The **Style Presets** feature in [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI") lets creators apply predefined or custom visual styles across [scenes](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes"), [shots](/docs/legacy-wiki/shots "Shots"), and [projects](/docs/legacy-wiki/projects "Projects").
Presets ensure consistency in tone, color, and atmosphere, whether you’re creating cinematic films, marketing ads, or [social media videos](/docs/legacy-wiki/social-media-videos "Social Media Videos").

## Key Capabilities

- **Cinematic Looks:** Apply LUTs and filmic styles (e.g., noir, sci-fi, documentary).
- **Artistic Styles:** Generate stylized visuals (e.g., anime, watercolor, oil painting).
- **Brand Consistency:** Apply corporate color palettes and typography.
- **Mood & Atmosphere:** Match lighting, contrast, and saturation to emotional tone.
- **Cross-Project Reuse:** Save presets to the [Asset Library](/docs/legacy-wiki/asset-library-2 "Asset Library") for reuse.

## How to Apply Style Presets

1. In the [Editor](/docs/legacy-wiki/editor "Editor"), select a scene or shot.
2. Open the **Style Presets** panel.
3. Choose from built-in presets or upload a custom LUT/visual style.
4. Apply to the active shot, full scene, or the entire project.
5. Preview results instantly and adjust intensity.

## Built-In Presets

- **Film & Cinema:** Cinematic, Noir, Warm Sunset, Cold Thriller
- **Marketing & Ads:** High Contrast, Brand Focus, Minimalist, Social-First
- **Artistic:** Watercolor, Comic Book, Anime, Surreal Dreamscape
- **Realism Variations:** Hyper-Real, Documentary, Natural Light
- **Experimental:** Glitch, Vaporwave, Cyberpunk

## Integration

- Works with [Google Veo 3](/docs/legacy-wiki/google-veo-3 "Google Veo 3") and [Runway Integration](/docs/legacy-wiki/runway "Runway") rendering engines.
- Applies across [Build Video](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video") timeline for consistency.
- Linked to [Color Grading](/docs/legacy-wiki/color-grading "Color Grading") for finer adjustments.
- Saved presets appear in [Asset Library](/docs/legacy-wiki/asset-library-2 "Asset Library").

## Best Practices

- Use consistent presets across an entire project for continuity.
- Combine presets with [lighting settings](/docs/legacy-wiki/lighting "Lighting") for full control.
- For branded work, create a custom preset that includes logos and brand LUTs.
- Test with draft renders before applying to final 4K exports.

## Troubleshooting

- **Preset looks different across shots** → Check lighting and camera settings in [Top-Down View](/docs/legacy-wiki/top-down-view "Top Down View").
- **Too strong effect** → Reduce preset intensity slider.
- **Preset not applied in export** → Ensure "Include Style Presets" is enabled in [Export settings](/docs/legacy-wiki/export-distribution "Export Distribution").
- **Custom LUT not loading** → Verify file format (.cube supported).
