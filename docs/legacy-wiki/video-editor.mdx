## Overview

The **Video Editor** in [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI") is the central workspace where creators bring together [scripts](/docs/legacy-wiki/scripts "Scripts"), [scenes](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes"), [digital actors](/docs/legacy-wiki/digital-actors "Digital Actors"), and [sets](/docs/legacy-wiki/sets "Sets") into polished video sequences.
It unifies writing, planning, sequencing, and previewing in a single environment, giving users both precision editing and AI-powered automation.

## Key Features

- **Multi-Pane Layout:** Switch between [Timeline View](/docs/legacy-wiki/timeline-view "Timeline View"), [Storyboards](/docs/legacy-wiki/storyboard-panels "Storyboard Panels"), [Top-Down View](/docs/legacy-wiki/top-down-view "Top Down View"), and [Script Editing](/docs/legacy-wiki/script-editor "Script Editor").
- **Drag-and-Drop Editing:** Arrange scenes, shots, and assets along the timeline.
- **Preview & Playback:** Watch low-res previews instantly without leaving the editor.
- **AI Integration:** One-click [AI Wizard](/docs/legacy-wiki/ai-wizard "AI Wizard") expansions for dialogue, pacing, and shot suggestions.
- **Collaboration Tools:** Inline comments, real-time editing, and version tracking.
- **Render Management:** Send clips or sequences directly to the [Render Queue](/docs/legacy-wiki/render-queue "Render Queue").

## Main Areas

The Video Editor workspace is divided into panels:

- **Script Pane** – Write or import dialogue and action.
- **Timeline Pane** – Arrange [scenes](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes"), [shots](/docs/legacy-wiki/shots "Shots"), and audio tracks.
- **Storyboard Pane** – Visualize storyboards or auto-generated shot panels.
- **Preview Pane** – Watch draft previews and rendered clips.
- **Top-Down View** – Place actors, cameras, props, and lights.

## Workflow

1. Start a [New Project](/docs/legacy-wiki/new-project "New Project") or open an existing one.
2. Draft your story in the [Script Editor](/docs/legacy-wiki/script-editor "Script Editor") or [import a script](/docs/legacy-wiki/import-script "Import Script").
3. Generate story beats and scenes with [AI Wizard](/docs/legacy-wiki/ai-wizard "AI Wizard").
4. Place actors, sets, and props in [Top-Down View](/docs/legacy-wiki/top-down-view "Top Down View").
5. Arrange shots and pacing in [Timeline View](/docs/legacy-wiki/timeline-view "Timeline View").
6. Add overlays, transitions, audio, and subtitles.
7. Preview clips in the [Preview Pane](/docs/legacy-wiki/preview "Preview").
8. Send to [Render Queue](/docs/legacy-wiki/render-queue "Render Queue") for draft or final rendering.

## Rendering & Export

- Draft previews allow quick iteration at low [credit cost](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System").
- Final sequences can be rendered in 720p, 1080p, or 4K via the [Rendering System](/docs/legacy-wiki/rendering-system "Rendering System").
- Export videos using [Export Distribution](/docs/legacy-wiki/export-distribution "Export Distribution") for TikTok, YouTube, or ProRes workflows.

## Collaboration

- Multiple team members can co-edit in real time.
- Commenting supports script lines, timeline sections, or storyboard panels.
- [Version Control](/docs/legacy-wiki/version-control "Version Control") and [Version History](/docs/legacy-wiki/version-history "Version History") ensure safe iteration.
- [Organization Management](/docs/legacy-wiki/organization-management "Organization Management") allows shared credits and project permissions.

## Best Practices

- Use draft previews frequently before high-res renders.
- Organize your timeline into acts and scenes for easier editing.
- Save manual [checkpoints](/docs/legacy-wiki/version-history "Version History") before big changes.
- Lock completed sequences to avoid accidental edits.

## Troubleshooting

- **Editor not loading** → Refresh or clear cache.
- **Preview not rendering** → Check [queue status](/docs/legacy-wiki/render-queue "Render Queue") and credit balance.
- **Timeline lagging** → Reduce active layers or lower preview resolution.
- **Assets missing** → Re-import via [Import B-Roll](/docs/legacy-wiki/import-broll "Import BRoll") or [script imports](/docs/legacy-wiki/import-script "Import Script").
