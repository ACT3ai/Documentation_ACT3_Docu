## Overview

The **Timeline** in [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI") is the central sequencing tool that organizes your [Scenes](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes"), [Shots](/docs/legacy-wiki/shots "Shots"), [B-Roll](/docs/legacy-wiki/b-roll "B-roll"), and audio into a structured video project.
It provides a visual timeline similar to traditional video editing software but enhanced with AI-powered features for faster scene assembly and preview generation.

## Key Features

- **Drag-and-Drop Editing:** Place [Scenes](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes"), [Shots](/docs/legacy-wiki/shots "Shots"), and [assets](/docs/legacy-wiki/media-library "Media Library") directly onto the timeline.
- **Multi-Track Support:** Arrange video, audio, and overlay elements across multiple synchronized tracks.
- **Zoom and Scrub:** Zoom in for frame-level accuracy or zoom out for a full project overview.
- **Block Sequencing:** Scenes and shots appear as blocks that can be reordered, trimmed, or extended.
- **AI-Assisted Sequencing:** Generate suggested orders for shots based on your script or [story arc](/docs/legacy-wiki/story-arcs "Story Arcs").
- **Real-Time Preview:** Play back any portion of the timeline without leaving the [Editor](/docs/legacy-wiki/editor "Editor").

## How to Use

1. Open the [Editor Workspace](/docs/legacy-wiki/editor "Editor") and select the **Timeline View**.
2. Drag [Shots](/docs/legacy-wiki/shots "Shots") or [Scenes](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes") from the [script editor](/docs/legacy-wiki/script-editor "Script Editor") or [Asset Library](/docs/legacy-wiki/asset-library-2 "Asset Library") into the timeline.
3. Arrange and trim clips using the handles on each block.
4. Add [B-Roll](/docs/legacy-wiki/b-roll "B-roll") or [Audio Tracks](/docs/legacy-wiki/audio-tracks "Audio Tracks") (music, dialogue, effects) onto their tracks.
5. Press **Play** to preview the selected section in real-time.

## Example Workflow

- Import a script via [Import Script](/docs/legacy-wiki/import-script "Import Script").
- Use the [AI Wizard](/docs/legacy-wiki/ai-wizard "AI Wizard") to generate acts and beats.
- Drop generated shots into the Timeline in order.
- Add background music from the [Asset Library](/docs/legacy-wiki/asset-library-2 "Asset Library").
- Preview the cut before sending it to [Build Video](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video").

## Tips

- Use color-coded blocks to distinguish [Scenes](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes"), [B-roll](/docs/legacy-wiki/b-roll "B-roll"), and audio layers.
- Group related shots together for easier navigation.
- For rapid prototyping, rely on low-res preview renders before committing [credits](https://wiki.act3ai.com/index.php?title=Credits&action=edit&redlink=1 "Credits (page does not exist)") to high-quality exports.
