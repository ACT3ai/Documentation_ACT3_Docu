## Overview

**Marketing Videos** in [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI") enable creators, teams, and businesses to rapidly produce branded content, explainer videos, and social media ads.
With [AI-driven story development](/docs/legacy-wiki/ai-wizard "AI Wizard"), [custom actors](/docs/legacy-wiki/digital-actors "Digital Actors"), and easy [video assembly](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video"), ACT 3 AI makes it possible to generate professional campaigns at scale, without traditional production overhead.

## Use Cases

- **Brand Awareness Campaigns** – Create short films and promos to establish brand identity.
- **Explainer Videos** – Turn complex topics into clear, engaging narratives.
- **Social Media Ads** – TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, optimized for 9:16 or 1:1.
- **Product Launch Videos** – Demonstrate features and benefits with AI-generated visuals.
- **Corporate Training Clips** – Standardized training and onboarding material.

## Workflow for Marketing Videos

### Step 1: Setup Project

- Create a [New Project](/docs/legacy-wiki/new-project "New Project") → Select type: Ad / Social Video / Explainer.
- Choose aspect ratio: 16:9 (YouTube), 9:16 (TikTok/Reels), or 1:1 (Instagram/Facebook).
- Define audience, tone, and target platforms.

### Step 2: Script & Story

- Use [AI Wizard](/docs/legacy-wiki/ai-wizard "AI Wizard") to generate key messages, beats, and call-to-actions.
- Draft or [import scripts](/docs/legacy-wiki/import-script "Import Script") with product highlights, testimonials, or brand values.
- Keep scenes short and direct for high engagement.

### Step 3: Visual Branding

- Select [Digital Actors](/docs/legacy-wiki/digital-actors "Digital Actors") that match brand demographics.
- Customize wardrobe, voice tone, and emotions to align with brand style.
- Add logos, text overlays, and motion graphics in [Build Video](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video").

### Step 4: Rendering & Preview

- Use [WAN AI Integration](/docs/legacy-wiki/wan "Wan") for fast previews during team collaboration.
- Render high-quality scenes with [Google Veo 3](/docs/legacy-wiki/google-veo-3 "Google Veo 3") or [Runway Integration](/docs/legacy-wiki/runway "Runway") depending on desired style.
- Test multiple variations of the same ad for A/B testing.

### Step 5: Export & Distribution

- Export optimized formats via [Export Distribution](/docs/legacy-wiki/export-distribution "Export Distribution") (MP4, MOV, ProRes).
- Publish directly to YouTube, TikTok, or Facebook Ads Manager.

## Best Practices

- **Hook first 3 seconds:** Grab attention early.
- **Keep under 30s:** Shorter content performs better on social platforms.
- **Brand consistency:** Use the same LUTs, fonts, and tone across campaigns.
- **A/B testing:** Render variations with different CTAs, actors, or styles.
- **Localization:** Export in multiple languages to reach wider markets.

## Troubleshooting

- Video too long → Split into shorter ads.
- Engagement low → Try alternate [characters](/docs/legacy-wiki/characters "Characters"), CTAs, or visual styles.
- Exported file too large → Re-render at 1080p instead of 4K.
- Ads rejected on social → Remove excessive text overlays or follow platform ad policies.
