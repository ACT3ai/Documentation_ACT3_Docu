## Overview

**ACT 3 AI** is a creative technology company dedicated to transforming the way stories are written, directed, and produced.
Our platform combines artificial intelligence with filmmaking workflows to make professional video creation accessible to everyone — from solo creators to global production studios.

## Our Mission

*“Write less, direct more.”*
We believe in giving creators the ability to focus on vision and storytelling while automating technical overhead.
ACT 3 AI empowers users to go from a one-line idea to a production-ready film, complete with scripts, digital actors, scenes, and rendered outputs.

## What We Offer

- End-to-end AI-assisted video production tools
- Intelligent story development with [AI Wizard](/docs/legacy-wiki/ai-wizard "AI Wizard")
- Customizable [digital actors](/docs/legacy-wiki/digital-actors "Digital Actors") and [characters](/docs/legacy-wiki/characters "Characters")
- Script writing and editing with [Script Editor](/docs/legacy-wiki/script-editor "Script Editor") and [Import Script](/docs/legacy-wiki/import-script "Import Script")
- Advanced integrations with rendering engines ([Google Veo 3](/docs/legacy-wiki/google-veo-3 "Google Veo 3"), [Runway Integration](/docs/legacy-wiki/runway "Runway"), [WAN AI Integration](/docs/legacy-wiki/wan "Wan"))
- Collaboration features for teams and organizations

## Who Uses ACT 3 AI

- [Social media creators](/docs/legacy-wiki/target-customers "Target Customers") (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram)
- Independent filmmakers and students
- Professional directors, producers, and screenwriters
- Marketing and creative agencies
- Animation and VFX teams
- Studio executives and investors

## Why Choose Us

- **Time savings:** Reduce hundreds of hours of pre-production into a few hours
- **Cost efficiency:** Eliminate location, equipment, and staffing costs
- **Creative scale:** Bring studio-grade workflows to solo creators and micro-studios
- **Human + AI blend:** You stay in control while AI accelerates the work

## Our Values

- Creativity first — technology serves the story.
- Collaboration — empowering teams and organizations.
- Transparency — clear [billing models](/docs/legacy-wiki/billing-model "Billing Model") and [credit usage](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System").
