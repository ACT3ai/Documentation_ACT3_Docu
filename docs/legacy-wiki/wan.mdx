## Overview

The **WAN AI Integration** in [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI") uses our proprietary wide-area-network AI service to generate video content optimized for low-latency collaboration.
It’s ideal for remote teams needing rapid previews and iterative revisions directly within the [Editor workspace](/docs/legacy-wiki/editor "Editor").

## Key Capabilities

- **Real-Time Previews:** Stream near-instant low-res clips during [script refinement](/docs/legacy-wiki/script-editing "Script Editing").
- **Adaptive Bitrate:** Dynamically adjust quality based on network conditions.
- **Collaborative Sessions:** Multiple users can co-generate and share live previews in a shared session.
- **Secure Streaming:** All data transfers are encrypted using end-to-end TLS.

## How to Use

1. In any [Editor](/docs/legacy-wiki/editor "Editor") view, click **AI → WAN AI** to open the WAN AI panel.
2. Enter your [shot prompt](/docs/legacy-wiki/shot-prompting "Shot Prompting") or select a [storyboard panel](/docs/legacy-wiki/storyboard-panels "Storyboard Panels").
3. Start the generation session; teammates with access can join via the session link.
4. Watch live updates as the AI refines the clip; provide inline feedback to steer further iterations.

## Pricing & Credits

- WAN AI uses [W-Credits](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System") billed by session length (flat rate per minute of generated video).
- Network-optimized previews incur a lower credit rate than full renders.

## Troubleshooting & Tips

- Ensure a stable internet connection—WAN AI adapts but works best above 5 Mbps.
- Use WAN AI for creative exploration; switch to [Google Veo 3](/docs/legacy-wiki/google-veo-3 "Google Veo 3") or [Runway Integration](/docs/legacy-wiki/runway "Runway") for final-quality exports.
- End sessions when idle to avoid unnecessary [credit usage](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System").
