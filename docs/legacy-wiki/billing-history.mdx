# Billing History

## Overview

The **Billing History** section in [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI") provides a detailed record of all subscription charges, credit purchases, and invoices associated with your account or organization.
It is accessible through [Account Settings → Billing](/docs/legacy-wiki/account-settings "Account Settings") and is available to account owners and admins in [Organization Management](/docs/legacy-wiki/organization-management "Organization Management").

## Key Features

- **Invoice Tracking:** View a complete history of transactions.
- **Subscription Records:** Track past and current [subscription plans](/docs/legacy-wiki/subscription-plan "Subscription Plan").
- **Credit Purchases:** See when additional [credits](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System") were bought.
- **Downloadable Invoices:** Export invoices in PDF or CSV format for accounting.
- **Filter & Search:** Find invoices by date range, type, or project.
- **Team Visibility:** Owners and admins can view shared organizational billing.

## How to Access

1. Log in to [https://app.act3ai.com](https://app.act3ai.com).
2. Click your profile icon → **Account Settings**.
3. Select **Billing → Billing History**.
4. Browse invoices, subscription renewals, and credit purchases.
5. Click any record to expand details or download a copy.

## Information Displayed

- Date of transaction.
- Payment method (credit card, PayPal, etc.).
- Amount charged.
- Type of charge (subscription, credits, adjustments).
- Invoice number (downloadable).
- Project or organization linked to the charge.

## Best Practices

- Download invoices monthly for accounting and tax purposes.
- Monitor credit purchases to track team usage.
- Assign billing responsibility to an [organization admin](/docs/legacy-wiki/organization-management "Organization Management").
- Review charges if you notice unexpected [credit consumption](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System").

## Troubleshooting

- **Missing invoice** → Refresh or check filters for the correct date.
- **Incorrect charge** → Contact support via [Contact Us](https://act3ai.com/contact).
- **Cannot download invoice** → Try again in a different browser or export as CSV.
- **Charges not matching credits** → Verify in [Credit Dashboard](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System").
