## Overview

The **Subscription Plan** system in [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI") offers tiered access with monthly credits, storage, and feature unlocks to match your workflow.
To choose or change a plan, visit: [ACT 3 Plans & Billing](https://app.act3ai.com/settings/plans/).

## Details by Plan

### FREE ( $0/mo )

- 800 credits/month; 0 credit rollover bank
- 50 MB cloud storage
- Personal use; videos include watermark
- Available only in the first 6 months of product use
- Always visible to community; some limitations with lower plan
- Includes: AI Image & Video Generation, Camera Controls, Character Casting & Animation

### STARTER ( $19/mo )

- 9,500 credits/month; 23,750 credit rollover bank
- 2 GB cloud storage
- Personal use; videos include watermark
- Available only in the first 6 months of product use
- Some limitations with lower plan
- Includes: AI Image & Video Generation, Camera Controls, Character Casting & Animation

### CREATOR ( $49/mo )

- 24,500 credits/month; 61,250 credit rollover bank
- 50 GB cloud storage
- Personal use; create motion videos **without watermark**
- Generate **3 jobs concurrently**
- Includes: AI Image & Video Generation, Camera Controls, Character Casting & Animation

### PRO ( $175/mo )

- 87,500 credits/month; 218,750 credit rollover bank
- 740 GB cloud storage
- Commercial use for target audiences up to 100k
- Generate **6 jobs concurrently**
- Create motion videos without watermark
- Includes: Power of Blender AI Story Controls; Power of Blender AI VFX Controls; Create Private 2D & 3D Sets; AI Image & Video Generation; Camera Controls; Character Casting & Animation

### ENTERPRISE ( Contact Us )

- High credits/month; custom storage
- 4K Video Creation; Priority Support
- Unlimited commercial use
- Generate **10+ jobs concurrently**
- Create motion videos without watermark
- Includes: Power of Blender AI Story Controls; Power of Blender AI VFX Controls; Create Private 2D & 3D Sets; AI Image & Video Generation; Camera Controls; Character Casting & Animation

## How to Change Your Plan

1. Open **Settings → Plans & Billing** in your account.
2. Compare tiers and select **Choose/Upgrade**.
3. Review [Billing Model](/docs/legacy-wiki/billing-model "Billing Model") and [Credit System](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System").
4. Changes apply immediately; credits and storage update with your tier.
