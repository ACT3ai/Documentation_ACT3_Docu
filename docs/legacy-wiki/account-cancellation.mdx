## Overview

The **Account Cancellation** process in [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI") allows you to permanently close your account, stop billing, and remove access to projects and services.
Before canceling, ensure you have downloaded all necessary [scripts](/docs/legacy-wiki/export-script "Export Script"), [video exports](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video"), or [documentation](/docs/legacy-wiki/documentation "Documentation"), since data may be deleted and cannot be recovered.

## How to Cancel Your Account

1. Log in to your ACT 3 AI account.
2. Go to **Settings → [Subscription & Billing](/docs/legacy-wiki/subscription-plan "Subscription Plan")**.
3. Select **Cancel Account**.
4. Review the confirmation prompt (this will include data deletion and billing notices).
5. Confirm by clicking **Permanently Cancel Account**.
6. You will receive a confirmation email once cancellation is complete.

## Important Notes

- **Billing:**

:   If you cancel mid-billing cycle, your account remains active until the end of the current paid period.
:   Unused [credits](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System") are forfeited at cancellation.

- **Projects & Data:**

:   All projects, [scenes](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes"), [digital actors](/docs/legacy-wiki/digital-actors "Digital Actors"), and [rendered outputs](/docs/legacy-wiki/render-queue "Render Queue") will be permanently deleted.
:   Make sure to [export](/docs/legacy-wiki/export-script "Export Script") your content before canceling.

- **Reactivation:**

:   To return later, you’ll need to create a new account and subscription plan.

## Alternatives to Cancellation

- Downgrade or change your [Subscription Plan](/docs/legacy-wiki/subscription-plan "Subscription Plan") instead of canceling.
- Pause your subscription if you plan to return later.
- Remove team members under [Organization Management](/docs/legacy-wiki/organization "Organization") instead of deleting your whole account.
