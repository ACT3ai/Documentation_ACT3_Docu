## Overview

The **Export Distribution** feature in [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI") manages how your final video projects are exported, packaged, and shared.
It supports industry-standard formats for editing, publishing, and direct distribution across platforms such as YouTube, TikTok, or internal review channels.

## Export Formats

- **Video Files:**
  - MP4 – lightweight, web-ready
  - MOV / ProRes – professional post-production
  - WebM – optimized for browsers
- **Editing Formats:**
  - EDL, AAF – for non-linear editors (Premiere, Avid)
  - USD, FBX – for 3D/VFX workflows
- **Resolutions:** 720p, 1080p, 4K (aspect ratios: 16:9, 9:16, 1:1)

## How to Export

1. From [Build Video](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video"), select **Export Options**.
2. Choose output format (MP4, ProRes, EDL, etc.).
3. Configure:
   1. Resolution and frame rate
   2. Subtitles / dubbing languages
   3. Delivery platform (YouTube, TikTok, Vimeo, etc.)
4. Click **Export**.
5. Monitor progress in the [Render Queue](/docs/legacy-wiki/render-queue "Render Queue").

## Distribution Options

- **Direct Publishing:** Upload automatically to YouTube, TikTok, or private cloud storage.
- **Team Review:** Share secure preview links within your [organization](/docs/legacy-wiki/organization "Organization").
- **Investor Deliverables:** Export pitch decks and annotated cuts.
- **Archiving:** Save master files in long-term storage.

## Best Practices

- Export test cuts in 720p/1080p before committing to 4K to save [credits](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System").
- Use platform-specific aspect ratios (9:16 for TikTok, 16:9 for YouTube).
- Generate subtitles for accessibility and SEO.
- Keep ProRes masters for professional editing, but distribute MP4 for sharing.

## Troubleshooting

- **Export failed** → Check [credit balance](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System") or free storage space.
- **Audio missing** → Ensure [audio tracks](/docs/legacy-wiki/audio-tracks "Audio Tracks") are enabled before export.
- **Wrong aspect ratio** → Verify [project settings](/docs/legacy-wiki/new-project "New Project") and [timeline layout](/docs/legacy-wiki/timeline "Timeline").
- **Subtitles not syncing** → Re-render with subtitle generation enabled.

## Credits & Billing

- Exporting consumes credits based on resolution and duration (see [Billing Model](/docs/legacy-wiki/billing-model "Billing Model")).
- Advanced outputs (ProRes, dubs, lookbooks) may incur additional charges.
