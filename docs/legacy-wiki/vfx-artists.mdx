## Overview

VFX and Blender Artists can integrate [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI") into their post-production pipeline to create **AI-generated assets, shots, and environments** that seamlessly blend with traditional 3D and compositing workflows.
By combining ACT 3 AI’s generative capabilities with tools like **Blender**, **After Effects**, and **Nuke**, artists can reduce manual asset creation time and focus on final polish and integration.

## Benefits for VFX Professionals

- **Faster Asset Creation:** Generate detailed backgrounds, props, or crowd scenes without starting from scratch.
- **Flexible Integration:** Export AI-generated content in industry-standard formats (e.g., **FBX**, **USD**, **EXR**).
- **Iterative Creativity:** Test multiple versions of an environment or effect before committing to final render.
- **Enhanced Storytelling:** Create fully fleshed-out scenes that enhance narrative depth.

## Key Features for VFX Artists

- **[Set Design](/docs/legacy-wiki/set-design "Set Design")** – Auto-generate realistic 2D and 3D environments.
- **[Cinematography](/docs/legacy-wiki/cinematography "Cinematography")** – Fine-tune camera paths, lighting, and lens settings for compositing.
- **[Build Video](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video")** – Render raw elements for integration into VFX pipelines.
- **[Import B-Roll](/docs/legacy-wiki/import-broll "Import BRoll")** – Bring in supplemental footage for compositing reference or layering.
- **[File Formats](/docs/legacy-wiki/file-formats "File Formats")** – Export in formats optimized for Blender, Unreal, or compositing tools.
- **[Digital Actors](/docs/legacy-wiki/digital-actors "Digital Actors")** – Create characters with consistent motion capture and facial performance data.

## Example Use Cases

- **Environment Generation:** Quickly produce alien planets, futuristic cities, or medieval villages for use as matte paintings or 3D backdrops.
- **Pre-Visualization:** Generate AI-powered previews to pitch concepts to directors or studios.
- **VFX Plate Enhancement:** Replace placeholder elements with high-quality, style-matched assets.
- **Crowd Simulation:** Use Digital Actors to fill stadiums, streets, or battlefields.

## Workflow for VFX Integration

1. Create or select a [New Project](/docs/legacy-wiki/new-project "New Project") for your sequence.
2. Use [Set Design](/docs/legacy-wiki/set-design "Set Design") to generate environments matching the project’s art direction.
3. Export AI-generated assets using [File Formats](/docs/legacy-wiki/file-formats "File Formats") compatible with Blender or your VFX suite.
4. Composite, enhance, or animate assets in your preferred VFX software.
5. Deliver final sequences through [Build Video](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video").
