## Overview

The **Google Veo 3 Integration** allows you to leverage Google’s Veo 3 video-generation API directly within [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI").
You can send shot-level prompts to Google Veo 3 and receive rendered video clips for use in your [Editor workspace](/docs/legacy-wiki/editor "Editor") or [Render Queue](/docs/legacy-wiki/render-queue "Render Queue").

## Key Capabilities

- **Prompt-to-Video:** Submit text descriptions of action, setting, and style; Google Veo 3 returns a short AI-generated clip.
- **Resolution & Frame Rate:** Choose output quality up to 1080p at 30 fps.
- **Style Presets:** Apply Google’s built-in artistic filters or supply custom style parameters.
- **Batch Requests:** Queue multiple prompts for parallel generation and monitor progress in the [Render Queue](/docs/legacy-wiki/render-queue "Render Queue").

## How to Use

1. In the [Editor](/docs/legacy-wiki/editor "Editor"), select a [shot](/docs/legacy-wiki/shots "Shots") or [scene](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes") and click **AI → Google Veo 3**.
2. Fill in your description, including action tags, [camera instructions](/docs/legacy-wiki/camera-instructions "Camera Instructions"), and desired mood settings.
3. Choose output settings (resolution, frame rate, style).
4. Click **Generate**: the request is sent to Google Veo 3, and the resulting clip appears in your Preview pane.
5. Drag accepted clips onto the [Timeline](/docs/legacy-wiki/timeline "Timeline") or add them to the [Render Queue](/docs/legacy-wiki/render-queue "Render Queue") for final export.

## Pricing & Credits

- Google Veo 3 tasks consume [V-Credits](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System") at a rate depending on resolution (e.g., 1 credit/sec at 1080p).
- The [Credits panel](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System") shows real-time cost estimates before you submit a request.

## Troubleshooting & Tips

- For best results, keep prompts under 100 words and focus on visual details.
- If you need longer takes, split the action into consecutive Veo 3 requests and stitch them in the [Timeline](/docs/legacy-wiki/timeline "Timeline").
- Review Google’s style documentation for available filters and recommended prompt structures.
