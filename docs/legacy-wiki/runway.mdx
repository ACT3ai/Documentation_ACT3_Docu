# Runway Integration

The **Runway Integration** in [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI") connects your projects to Runway ML’s advanced generative video models, enabling high-quality, stylized, and experimental outputs directly within the [Editor workspace](/docs/legacy-wiki/editor "Editor").
This integration is ideal for creative exploration, stylized video creation, and fast iteration during pre-production.

## Key Capabilities

- **Text-to-Video:** Generate clips directly from [shot prompts](/docs/legacy-wiki/shot-prompting "Shot Prompting") or scene descriptions.
- **Style & Model Selection:** Access multiple Runway models, including those optimized for cinematic realism, animation, and abstract effects.
- **Frame Interpolation:** Smooth motion between generated frames for a more natural look.
- **Masking & Inpainting:** Replace or enhance specific regions in a shot while keeping the rest intact.

## How to Use

1. In the [Editor](/docs/legacy-wiki/editor "Editor"), select a [scene](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes") or [shot](/docs/legacy-wiki/shots "Shots") and click **AI → Runway**.
2. Enter your description, including action tags, [camera instructions](/docs/legacy-wiki/camera-instructions "Camera Instructions"), and any [style presets](/docs/legacy-wiki/style-presets "Style Presets").
3. Choose your [Runway model](/docs/legacy-wiki/runway-model "Runway Model"), resolution, and frame rate.
4. Click **Generate**: the clip will appear in your Preview pane for review.
5. Add approved clips to the [Timeline](/docs/legacy-wiki/timeline "Timeline") or [Render Queue](/docs/legacy-wiki/render-queue "Render Queue").

## Pricing & Credits

- Runway generations consume [R-Credits](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System") based on clip length, resolution, and selected model complexity.
- Advanced features like masking and frame interpolation may incur additional costs.

## Troubleshooting & Tips

- Keep prompts under 120 words for faster generation and better focus.
- For complex visual effects, break your request into multiple short prompts.
- Use masking to refine only problem areas instead of regenerating full clips.
- Combine Runway’s stylization with [Google Veo 3](/docs/legacy-wiki/google-veo-3 "Google Veo 3") for hybrid results — Veo for realism, Runway for artistic effects.
