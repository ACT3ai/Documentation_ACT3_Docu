## Overview

The **Music Sync** feature in [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI") allows you to synchronize background music, voice-over, and sound effects with your generated videos. This ensures timing, mood, and pacing are aligned with your [Timeline](/docs/legacy-wiki/timeline "Timeline") and [Storyboard Panels](/docs/legacy-wiki/storyboard-panels "Storyboard Panels").

## Key Capabilities

- **Import Audio:** Upload licensed tracks, sound effects, or custom voice-overs into the [Media Library](/docs/legacy-wiki/media-library "Media Library").
- **Auto-Sync:** AI analyzes beat, rhythm, and transitions, auto-aligning them with [scenes](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes") and [shots](/docs/legacy-wiki/shots "Shots").
- **Manual Controls:** Drag audio clips onto the [Timeline](/docs/legacy-wiki/timeline "Timeline") for fine-grained editing.
- **Multiple Tracks:** Layer dialogue, background score, and effects together for cinematic sound design.
- **Adaptive Length:** AI trims or loops audio to fit video duration.

## How to Use

1. Go to the [Editor](/docs/legacy-wiki/editor "Editor") and open the **Audio & Music Panel**.
2. Select **Import Music** to add files from your system or [Media Library](/docs/legacy-wiki/media-library "Media Library").
3. Drag the track onto the [Timeline](/docs/legacy-wiki/timeline "Timeline") under the music/audio layer.
4. Use **Sync with Video** to align beats with scene cuts automatically.
5. Preview in the [Preview Window](/docs/legacy-wiki/preview "Preview") and adjust timing with manual keyframes.

## Pricing & Credits

- Music sync operations (auto-beat alignment, adaptive length) consume small fractions of credits.
- Importing and placing raw audio files does not consume credits.

## Troubleshooting & Tips

- Use high-quality WAV or MP3 files for best results.
- If sync drifts, manually re-align by dragging the audio waveform on the [Timeline](/docs/legacy-wiki/timeline "Timeline").
- Avoid overlapping too many tracks unless necessary—this can increase render time.
