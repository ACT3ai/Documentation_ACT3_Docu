## Overview

**Social Media Videos** in [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI") are optimized for platforms like TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, and Facebook.
The platform streamlines every step — from [project setup](/docs/legacy-wiki/new-project "New Project") and [script generation](/docs/legacy-wiki/ai-wizard "AI Wizard") to [editing](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video"), [rendering](/docs/legacy-wiki/rendering-system "Rendering System"), and [exporting](/docs/legacy-wiki/export-distribution "Export Distribution") in platform-ready formats.

## Supported Platforms

- **TikTok** – 15s–60s vertical 9:16 videos, trend-driven content.
- **YouTube Shorts** – Up to 60s vertical videos, focused on retention and discovery.
- **Instagram Reels** – Short vertical clips with overlays, trending sounds.
- **Facebook Video Ads** – 9:16 vertical or 1:1 square formats for social ads.
- **LinkedIn Videos** – Professional explainers, testimonials, and training in 16:9 or 1:1.

## Workflow for Social Media Videos

### Step 1: Create a Project

- Go to [New Project](/docs/legacy-wiki/new-project "New Project") → Select “Social Media Video”.
- Choose aspect ratio:
  - 9:16 – TikTok, Reels, Shorts
  - 1:1 – Instagram/Facebook square ads
  - 16:9 – YouTube horizontal
- Define audience, tone, and target platform.

### Step 2: Script & Messaging

- Use [AI Wizard](/docs/legacy-wiki/ai-wizard "AI Wizard") to generate catchy intros, beats, and CTAs.
- Draft or [import scripts](/docs/legacy-wiki/import-script "Import Script") that emphasize short, punchy dialogue.
- Focus on strong hooks in the first 3–5 seconds.

### Step 3: Visual Design

- Use [Digital Actors](/docs/legacy-wiki/digital-actors "Digital Actors") or avatars that fit your target demographic.
- Add text overlays, emojis, and logos to drive brand recognition.
- Build dynamic settings with [Set Design](/docs/legacy-wiki/set-design "Set Design") and [Top-Down View](/docs/legacy-wiki/top-down-view "Top Down View").

### Step 4: Rendering

- Generate previews with [WAN AI Integration](/docs/legacy-wiki/wan "Wan") for fast team feedback.
- Render final clips with [Google Veo 3](/docs/legacy-wiki/google-veo-3 "Google Veo 3") for realism or [Runway Integration](/docs/legacy-wiki/runway "Runway") for stylized content.
- Keep most exports at 1080p for speed and compatibility.

### Step 5: Export & Publish

- Export via [Export Distribution](/docs/legacy-wiki/export-distribution "Export Distribution") as MP4.
- Auto-generate subtitles & captions for accessibility.
- Create multi-language versions to maximize reach.
- Publish directly to TikTok, YouTube Shorts, or Instagram.

## Best Practices

- **Hook early:** First 3 seconds determine audience retention.
- **Keep it short:** 15–30s performs best on TikTok/Reels.
- **Format correctly:** Always use 9:16 for vertical-first platforms.
- **Use trends:** Incorporate popular sounds, hashtags, and styles.
- **Brand consistency:** Keep fonts, colors, and tone aligned across campaigns.

## Troubleshooting

- **Video cropped incorrectly** → Verify aspect ratio in [project settings](/docs/legacy-wiki/new-project "New Project").
- **Low engagement** → Shorten script, add stronger CTAs, or test variations.
- **Export too large** → Use 1080p instead of 4K for social platforms.
- **Subtitles misaligned** → Regenerate with subtitle sync enabled.
