## Overview

The **Generative Workflow Pipeline** in [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI") describes how projects move from initial ideas or [imported scripts](/docs/legacy-wiki/import-script "Import Script") to fully rendered videos using AI models.
This pipeline connects writing, planning, casting, rendering, and distribution into one continuous loop of human creativity and AI assistance.

## Pipeline Stages

### 1. Ideation & Project Setup

- Start a [New Project](/docs/legacy-wiki/new-project "New Project") with title, description, and format (film, ad, TikTok, concept test).
- Define aspect ratio and length.
- Use [AI Wizard](/docs/legacy-wiki/ai-wizard "AI Wizard") to expand a one-line idea into structured loglines, acts, and beats.

### 2. Script Development

- Write in the [Script Editor](/docs/legacy-wiki/script-editor "Script Editor") or [import a script](/docs/legacy-wiki/import-script "Import Script") from [Final Draft](/docs/legacy-wiki/final-draft "Final Draft").
- AI can expand outlines into scenes and dialogues.
- Use [Export Script](/docs/legacy-wiki/export-script "Export Script") for collaboration with writers.

### 3. Characters & Casting

- Define [Characters](/docs/legacy-wiki/characters "Characters") with names, roles, and backstories.
- Assign [Digital Actors](/docs/legacy-wiki/digital-actors "Digital Actors") with wardrobe, voices, and emotional tone.
- Optional: Capture live movement via [Motion Capture Integration](/docs/legacy-wiki/motion-capture "Motion Capture").

### 4. Visual Planning

- Use [Storyboards & Panels](/docs/legacy-wiki/storyboard-panels "Storyboard Panels") for shot previews.
- Place [actors](/docs/legacy-wiki/digital-actors "Digital Actors"), cameras, and props in [Top-Down View](/docs/legacy-wiki/top-down-view "Top Down View").
- Sequence everything in the [Timeline editor](/docs/legacy-wiki/timeline "Timeline").

### 5. Generative Rendering

- Choose rendering engines based on style:
  - [Google Veo 3 Integration](/docs/legacy-wiki/google-veo-3 "Google Veo 3") – realism & cinematic
  - [Runway Integration](/docs/legacy-wiki/runway "Runway") – stylized, experimental
  - [WAN AI Integration](/docs/legacy-wiki/wan "Wan") – real-time collaboration previews
- Generate draft previews or final-quality renders.
- Monitor costs in the [Credit System](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System") panel.

### 6. Assembly & Editing

- Combine AI clips, [B-Roll](/docs/legacy-wiki/import-broll "Import BRoll"), and audio in [Build Video](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video").
- Add transitions, overlays, [color grading](/docs/legacy-wiki/color-grading "Color Grading"), and [sound design](/docs/legacy-wiki/audio-tracks "Audio Tracks").
- Lock approved scenes to prevent rework.

### 7. Export & Distribution

- Send final sequences to the [Render Queue](/docs/legacy-wiki/render-queue "Render Queue").
- Export via [Export Distribution](/docs/legacy-wiki/export-distribution "Export Distribution") to MP4, ProRes, or social-optimized formats.
- Attach subtitles, multi-language dubs, and thumbnails.
- Share directly to YouTube, TikTok, or internal review.

### 8. Feedback & Iteration

- Gather notes via [collaborator comments](/docs/legacy-wiki/collaboration "Collaboration").
- Regenerate scenes or re-edit sequences.
- Update the pipeline in real-time until final approval.

## Best Practices

- Use [draft previews](/docs/legacy-wiki/draft-previews "Draft Previews") before high-resolution renders.
- Keep consistent LUTs/lighting across scenes for visual continuity.
- Lock assets once finalized to avoid credit waste.
- Integrate feedback loops early to save time.

## Example Pipeline Flow

Idea → [AI Wizard](/docs/legacy-wiki/ai-wizard "AI Wizard") → [Script](/docs/legacy-wiki/script-editor "Script Editor") → [Characters & Digital Actors](/docs/legacy-wiki/characters "Characters") → [Storyboards](/docs/legacy-wiki/storyboard-panels "Storyboard Panels") → [Timeline](/docs/legacy-wiki/timeline "Timeline") → [AI Rendering](/docs/legacy-wiki/ai-rendered-footage "AI Rendered Footage") → [Build Video](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video") → [Render Queue](/docs/legacy-wiki/render-queue "Render Queue") → [Export Distribution](/docs/legacy-wiki/export-distribution "Export Distribution")
