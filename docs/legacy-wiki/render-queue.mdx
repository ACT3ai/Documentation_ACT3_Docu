## Overview

The **Render Queue** in [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI") manages all rendering tasks — from draft previews to final 4K exports.
It allows you to schedule, monitor, and prioritize jobs, making rendering efficient for both solo creators and collaborative teams.

## Key Functions

- **Job Scheduling:** Queue multiple [scenes](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes"), [shots](/docs/legacy-wiki/shots "Shots"), or full [videos](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video") for processing.
- **Progress Tracking:** Monitor render status, estimated time, and credit usage.
- **Batch Processing:** Render overnight or in parallel to save time.
- **Prioritization:** Reorder or pause jobs depending on urgency.
- **Collaboration:** Team members can submit renders, with visibility across the organization.

## How to Use

1. From the [Editor workspace](/docs/legacy-wiki/editor "Editor") or [Build Video](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video") page, select **Render → Add to Queue**.
2. Choose render settings (resolution, quality, engine).
3. Confirm estimated [credit cost](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System").
4. Submit the job — it appears in the Render Queue dashboard.
5. Track progress in real time, cancel or reprioritize if needed.

## Render Settings

- **Resolution:** Draft, 720p, 1080p, 4K
- **Engines:**
  - [Google Veo 3](/docs/legacy-wiki/google-veo-3 "Google Veo 3") – cinematic realism
  - [Runway Integration](/docs/legacy-wiki/runway "Runway") – stylized/experimental
  - [WAN AI Integration](/docs/legacy-wiki/wan "Wan") – collaborative previews
- **Quality Levels:** Draft (low-cost), Medium, High, Ultra (4K)
- **Output Formats:** MP4, MOV/ProRes, WebM

## Managing the Queue

- Drag jobs to reprioritize.
- Pause, resume, or cancel active tasks.
- View per-job credit usage and resolution.
- Completed jobs are saved for download in [Export Distribution](/docs/legacy-wiki/export-distribution "Export Distribution").

## Credits & Billing

- Each job consumes credits based on resolution, engine, and duration (see [Billing Model](/docs/legacy-wiki/billing-model "Billing Model")).
- Draft previews are low-cost; 4K exports consume more.
- Admins in [Organization accounts](/docs/legacy-wiki/organization-management "Organization Management") can view usage by user/project.

## Troubleshooting

- **Job stuck in queue** → Try canceling and re-queuing.
- **Credits insufficient** → Top up via [Subscription Plan](/docs/legacy-wiki/subscription-plan "Subscription Plan").
- **Output missing audio/video** → Recheck [project assembly](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video") settings.
- **Slow rendering** → Heavy loads may delay jobs; schedule overnight or lower resolution.

## Best Practices

- Use draft previews first to validate timing before high-res renders.
- Batch similar jobs together to save time.
- Keep the queue organized by project or client.
- Schedule overnight to avoid bottlenecks during active work hours.
