## Overview

The **Community** section in [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI") is where creators, filmmakers, and AI video enthusiasts come together to share projects, exchange ideas, and collaborate.
It acts as both a showcase for completed [videos](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video") and a hub for discussion on techniques, [visual styles](/docs/legacy-wiki/visual-style "Visual Style"), and best practices.

By participating in the Community, you can receive valuable feedback, discover inspiration from other members, and build your creative network.

## Key Features

- **Project Showcase:** Publish your completed [scenes](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes"), [shots](/docs/legacy-wiki/shots "Shots"), or full videos for others to view.
- **Feedback & Reviews:** Receive constructive feedback from other creators and provide insights on their work.
- **Collaboration Requests:** Find partners for co-producing videos, writing scripts, or designing [sets](/docs/legacy-wiki/set-design "Set Design").
- **Tutorial Sharing:** Post guides, walkthroughs, or [tutorial links](/docs/legacy-wiki/tutorials "Tutorials") to help the community grow.
- **Trending Content:** See the most popular or most-discussed projects each week.

## How to Participate

1. Go to the **Community** section from the sidebar.
2. Click **Upload Project** to share your work.
3. Add a title, description, tags, and optionally a [style profile](/docs/legacy-wiki/visual-style "Visual Style") or script excerpt.
4. Engage with other members by commenting on their posts or sending direct collaboration invites.
5. Follow topics or creators to receive updates in your feed.

## Best Practices

- Provide specific and constructive feedback to help other creators improve.
- Tag your projects with genre, style, and tool keywords for better discoverability.
- Use the [AI Wizard](/docs/legacy-wiki/ai-wizard "AI Wizard") and [cinematography tools](/docs/legacy-wiki/cinematography "Cinematography") to experiment before sharing.
- Respect the Terms of Service and community guidelines.

## Benefits of Joining

- Gain exposure for your creative work.
- Learn from diverse styles and workflows.
- Build lasting relationships with other filmmakers and AI content creators.
- Access exclusive [documentation](/docs/legacy-wiki/documentation "Documentation") and early feature previews shared in the community.
