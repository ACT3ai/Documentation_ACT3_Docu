## Overview

The **Timeline View** in [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI") is the core workspace for arranging and editing [scenes](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes"), [shots](/docs/legacy-wiki/shots "Shots"), and assets in chronological order.
It provides a time-based editing interface where you can adjust pacing, synchronize [audio](/docs/legacy-wiki/audio-tracks "Audio Tracks"), and preview your project before final rendering.

## Key Features

- **Drag-and-Drop Editing:** Move [scenes](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes") and [shots](/docs/legacy-wiki/shots "Shots") directly from the [script editor](/docs/legacy-wiki/script-editor "Script Editor") into the timeline.
- **Zoom Controls:** Zoom in for frame-level precision or zoom out for full-scene overview.
- **Layering:** Stack video, audio, overlays, and [actor tracks](/docs/legacy-wiki/digital-actors "Digital Actors") for complex sequences.
- **Preview Playback:** Play full sequences or selected sections.
- **Locking & Muting:** Lock tracks to prevent edits or mute layers during playback.
- **Credit Estimation:** View estimated [credit usage](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System") for rendering sections.

## Workflow

1. Open the [Editor workspace](/docs/legacy-wiki/editor "Editor") and switch to Timeline View.
2. Import or draft content via [Import Script](/docs/legacy-wiki/import-script "Import Script") or [AI Wizard](/docs/legacy-wiki/ai-wizard "AI Wizard").
3. Drag script-generated blocks onto the timeline.
4. Arrange shots, adjust timing, and add transitions or overlays.
5. Insert [audio tracks](/docs/legacy-wiki/audio-tracks "Audio Tracks"), subtitles, or B-roll.
6. Preview sequences before sending to the [Render Queue](/docs/legacy-wiki/render-queue "Render Queue").

## Integration

- Linked to [Storyboards & Panels](/docs/legacy-wiki/storyboard-panels "Storyboard Panels") for visual alignment.
- Works with [Set Design](/docs/legacy-wiki/set-design "Set Design") and [Top-Down View](/docs/legacy-wiki/top-down-view "Top Down View") for camera/actor placement.
- Connected to [Build Video](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video") for final assembly.
- Sends output jobs to the [Rendering System](/docs/legacy-wiki/rendering-system "Rendering System") and [Render Queue](/docs/legacy-wiki/render-queue "Render Queue").

## Best Practices

- Keep timeline organized with labeled tracks.
- Use **draft previews** before committing to 4K exports to save credits.
- Group shots by [scene](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes") or [act](/docs/legacy-wiki/acts "Acts") for easier navigation.
- Regularly lock finalized sequences to avoid accidental changes.

## Troubleshooting

- **Timeline not updating** → Refresh or re-sync from [script editor](/docs/legacy-wiki/script-editor "Script Editor").
- **Audio out of sync** → Adjust manually or re-align in [Build Video](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video").
- **Performance lag** → Lower preview resolution or mute unused layers.
- **Credit usage too high** → Preview shorter sections before full render.

## Example Use Cases

- Editing a 30-second TikTok with jump cuts and overlays.
- Assembling a full 5-minute short film with dialogue, B-roll, and effects.
- Organizing multiple social ads within a single project timeline.
