## Overview

The **Billing Model** for [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI") is credit-based, with different subscription tiers designed for personal creators, professional filmmakers, and enterprises.
Credits are consumed when generating AI video, images, and assets. Each plan includes cloud storage, credit rollover, and advanced feature access.

## Credit System

- Credits are consumed for rendering, generation, and exports (see [Credit\_System](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System")).
- Higher plans include larger rollover banks to save unused credits.
- Check your balance under **Billing → Credit Usage** in your dashboard.

## Upgrading

- Plans can be upgraded anytime under [Subscription Plan](/docs/legacy-wiki/subscription-plan "Subscription Plan").
- Unused credits transfer into your rollover bank (up to plan limits).
- Enterprise customers can negotiate custom features.
