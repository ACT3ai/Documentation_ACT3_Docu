# ACT 3 AI – Product Documentation

Welcome to the official documentation for **[ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI")**, the end‑to‑end platform for AI‑assisted filmmaking and video creation — from the first spark of an idea to a finished, production‑ready export.

*Write less, direct more.*

---

## What is ACT 3 AI?

ACT 3 AI helps you turn prompts or scripts into complete visual stories. You’ll outline acts and beats with [AI Wizard](/docs/legacy-wiki/ai-wizard "AI Wizard"), cast [Digital Actors](/docs/legacy-wiki/digital-actors "Digital Actors"), plan shots in the [Editor](/docs/legacy-wiki/editor "Editor"), and render scenes with model integrations like [Google Veo 3](/docs/legacy-wiki/google-veo-3 "Google Veo 3"), [Runway](/docs/legacy-wiki/runway "Runway"), and [WAN AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/wan "Wan").
If you’re new, jump to [Getting Started](/docs/legacy-wiki/getting-started "Getting Started") first.

---

## Start Here (First 10 Minutes)

1. [Getting Started Guide](/docs/legacy-wiki/getting-started "Getting Started") – onboarding, project setup, your first render.
2. [New Project](/docs/legacy-wiki/new-project "New Project") – title, genre, project type, aspect ratio, organization.
3. [AI Wizard](/docs/legacy-wiki/ai-wizard "AI Wizard") – logline → acts → beats → scenes.
4. [Characters](/docs/legacy-wiki/characters "Characters") and [Digital Actors](/docs/legacy-wiki/digital-actors "Digital Actors") – define roles, cast AI performers.
5. [Import Script](/docs/legacy-wiki/import-script "Import Script") or write in [Script Editor](/docs/legacy-wiki/script-editor "Script Editor").
6. Visual planning: [Storyboard & Panels](/docs/legacy-wiki/storyboard-panels "Storyboard Panels") · [Top‑Down View](/docs/legacy-wiki/top-down-view "Top Down View").
7. Generate: [Google\_Veo\_3](/docs/legacy-wiki/google-veo-3 "Google Veo 3") · [Runway](/docs/legacy-wiki/runway "Runway") · [WAN AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/wan "Wan").
8. Assemble & export: [Build Video](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video") and [Render Queue](/docs/legacy-wiki/render-queue "Render Queue").

## Billing & Credits (At a Glance)

The platform uses a credit model to keep costs predictable and transparent. See [Billing\_Model](/docs/legacy-wiki/billing-model "Billing Model") for plan details and [Credit\_System](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System") for usage tips.

| Task | Typical Credits | Tip |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Draft preview (10s, 1080p) | 1–2 | Use previews early; avoid re‑rendering finals. |
| Final render (10s, 1080p) | ~5 | Lock scenes once approved. |
| [Veo 3](/docs/legacy-wiki/google-veo-3 "Google Veo 3") (10s, 1080p) | ~6 | Keep prompts precise (&lt;100 words&gt;). |
| [WAN AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/wan "Wan") live session (per min) | ~2–3 | End idle sessions to save credits. |

- Plan/upgrade in [Subscription Plan](/docs/legacy-wiki/subscription-plan "Subscription Plan").
- Track consumption in the Credits panel (see [Credit\_System](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System")).
