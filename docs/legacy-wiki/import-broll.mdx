## Overview

The **Import B-Roll** feature in [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI") allows you to bring in supplemental footage—such as establishing shots, atmospheric clips, or cutaway visuals—directly into your [video project](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video").
B-Roll is often used to add context, enhance storytelling, and improve pacing between [scenes](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes") without changing the core [script](/docs/legacy-wiki/script-editor "Script Editor").

You can import pre-recorded B-Roll from your local drive, stock libraries, or cloud storage, then integrate it into your [Timeline](/docs/legacy-wiki/timeline "Timeline") alongside AI-generated footage.

## Key Capabilities

- **Multiple Sources:** Import from local files, stock libraries, or connected cloud drives (Google Drive, Dropbox).
- **Format Support:** Accepts common formats (MP4, MOV, ProRes, WebM) up to 4K.
- **Metadata Retention:** Keeps original frame rate, resolution, and timecode for syncing.
- **Direct Timeline Placement:** Drag imported clips directly into [Timeline](/docs/legacy-wiki/timeline "Timeline") or [Storyboard Panels](/docs/legacy-wiki/storyboard-panels "Storyboard Panels").
- **AI-Assisted Matching:** Suggests B-Roll placement based on [scene context](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes") and [shot prompts](/docs/legacy-wiki/shot-prompting "Shot Prompting").

## How to Import B-Roll

1. Open your project in the [Editor](/docs/legacy-wiki/editor "Editor").
2. Select **File → Import → B-Roll** or click the **Import B-Roll** button in the [Media Library](/docs/legacy-wiki/media-library "Media Library").
3. Choose your source (local upload, cloud storage, or stock library).
4. Review and confirm import settings (resolution, frame rate, trimming).
5. The imported clips will appear in the [Media Library](/docs/legacy-wiki/media-library "Media Library") for use in [Timeline](/docs/legacy-wiki/timeline "Timeline").

## Using B-Roll in Your Project

- Drag clips from the [Media Library](/docs/legacy-wiki/media-library "Media Library") into empty spaces or above [scenes](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes") in [Timeline](/docs/legacy-wiki/timeline "Timeline").
- Adjust timing to match script beats or [music sync](/docs/legacy-wiki/music-sync "Music Sync").
- Layer B-Roll over AI-generated shots to create transitions or enhance mood.
- Use color grading tools to match B-Roll style to your primary footage.

## Best Practices

- Keep clips short (5–10 seconds) for pacing.
- Match lighting, color tone, and aspect ratio with your main footage.
- Use B-Roll to visually explain or emphasize narration or dialogue.
- Consider AI-assisted scene matching to speed up placement.

## Troubleshooting

- **Wrong aspect ratio** → Use the crop & resize tool before adding to Timeline.
- **Audio mismatch** → Mute B-Roll audio or sync with audio tracks.
- **Laggy playback** → Convert large ProRes files to proxy format before import.

## Credits & Storage

- Importing B-Roll does not consume [credits](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System").
- AI-assisted matching or style conversion may consume a small number of credits.
- Storage usage counts toward your [plan limits](/docs/legacy-wiki/subscription-plan "Subscription Plan").
