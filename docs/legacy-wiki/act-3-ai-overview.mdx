## What is ACT 3 AI?

**ACT 3 AI** is an end-to-end AI-powered filmmaking platform that transforms ideas, scripts, and storyboards into production-ready video content.
It unifies creative writing, directing, casting, cinematography, and rendering into one seamless workflow, allowing creators to focus on storytelling while AI handles technical overhead.

*“Write less, direct more.”* — ACT 3 AI Mission

## Who Uses ACT 3 AI

ACT 3 AI serves a wide spectrum of creators:

- Social media creators (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram)
- Indie filmmakers and students
- TV/movie professionals (writers, directors, producers)
- Marketing and creative agencies
- VFX and animation studios
- Studio executives and investors

## Core Capabilities

### ✍️ Writing & Story Development

- Expand ideas into structured [scripts](/docs/legacy-wiki/script-editor "Script Editor")
- Convert outlines or books into [beats](/docs/legacy-wiki/story-beats "Story Beats"), [scenes](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes"), and [shots](/docs/legacy-wiki/shots "Shots")
- Generate arcs and structure with [AI Wizard](/docs/legacy-wiki/ai-wizard "AI Wizard")

### 🎬 Directing & Visualization

- Plan acts, scenes, and beats with visual tools
- Use [Storyboards & Panels](/docs/legacy-wiki/storyboard-panels "Storyboard Panels") and [Top-Down View](/docs/legacy-wiki/top-down-view "Top Down View") for camera blocking
- Preview ideas in real time within the [Editor workspace](/docs/legacy-wiki/editor "Editor")

### 🎥 Cinematography

- Control camera moves, lenses, ISO, depth of field, and framing
- Apply LUTs and mood presets for cinematic quality
- Manage lighting and time-of-day settings

### 👥 Casting & Performance

- Create and customize [characters](/docs/legacy-wiki/characters "Characters")
- Assign them to [Digital Actors](/docs/legacy-wiki/digital-actors "Digital Actors") with wardrobe, style, and emotion settings
- Capture motion via iPhone or external mocap devices

### 🏗️ Set Design & Assets

- Generate 2D/3D sets automatically or import from Blender
- Reuse locations and props across episodes or projects
- Add [B-Roll](/docs/legacy-wiki/import-broll "Import BRoll") and stock footage

### ⚡ Rendering & Output

- Generate AI clips with [Google Veo 3](/docs/legacy-wiki/google-veo-3 "Google Veo 3"), [Runway Integration](/docs/legacy-wiki/runway "Runway"), or [WAN AI Integration](/docs/legacy-wiki/wan "Wan")
- Assemble final projects in [Build Video](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video")
- Queue jobs in [Render Queue](/docs/legacy-wiki/render-queue "Render Queue")
- Export in MP4, ProRes, 1080p, or 4K with subtitles, dubs, and pitch decks

## Technology Behind ACT 3 AI

ACT 3 AI orchestrates state-of-the-art video generation models, including:

- [Google Veo 3](/docs/legacy-wiki/google-veo-3 "Google Veo 3")
- [Runway](/docs/legacy-wiki/runway "Runway")
- [WAN AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/wan "Wan")
- Stable Diffusion XL (SDXL)
- Flux, ComfyUI, Hunyuan

## Why ACT 3 AI?

- **Time savings:** Reduce 80–200 hours of pre-production to just a few hours
- **Cost savings:** Eliminate location, crew, and equipment costs
- **Scalability:** Solo creators and small studios can achieve Hollywood-level workflows
- **Control:** Human-in-the-loop editing ensures creative vision is preserved
