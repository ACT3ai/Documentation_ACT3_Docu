## Overview

The **Create Account** process registers you for the [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI") platform, giving you access to all features including [project creation](/docs/legacy-wiki/new-project "New Project"), [AI Wizard](/docs/legacy-wiki/ai-wizard "AI Wizard"), [Digital Actors](/docs/legacy-wiki/digital-actors "Digital Actors"), and [Build Video](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video").
Accounts are free to create and can be upgraded later via the [Subscription Plan](/docs/legacy-wiki/subscription-plan "Subscription Plan") page.

## How to Create an Account

1. Go to the ACT 3 AI web app: [https://app.act3ai.com](https://app.act3ai.com)
2. Click **Sign Up** on the login page.
3. Enter your details:
   1. Full Name
   2. Email Address
   3. Password (minimum 8 characters)
4. Verify your email address via the confirmation link.
5. Log in with your new credentials.

## Organization vs Personal Accounts

- **Personal Account** – for individual creators working on personal projects.
- **Organization Account** – shared account for teams, with role management and shared [credit pools](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System").

## Account Features

- Access the [Dashboard](/docs/legacy-wiki/dashboard "Dashboard") for projects and settings.
- Start a [New Project](/docs/legacy-wiki/new-project "New Project") or [Import Script](/docs/legacy-wiki/import-script "Import Script").
- Use AI integrations: [Google\_Veo\_3](/docs/legacy-wiki/google-veo-3 "Google Veo 3"), [Runway](/docs/legacy-wiki/runway "Runway"), [WAN AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/wan "Wan").
- Manage [billing](/docs/legacy-wiki/billing-model "Billing Model") and [credits](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System").
- Upgrade or downgrade via [Subscription Plan](/docs/legacy-wiki/subscription-plan "Subscription Plan").

## Managing Your Account

- Update your profile under [Account Settings](/docs/legacy-wiki/account-settings "Account Settings").
- Reset your password if forgotten.
- Add or remove team members if using an [Organization Account](/docs/legacy-wiki/organization "Organization").
- Review invoices and receipts in [Billing History](/docs/legacy-wiki/billing-history "Billing History").

## Troubleshooting

- **Didn’t receive confirmation email?** → Check spam/junk folder or request resend.
- **Password reset not working?** → Use the [reset form](https://app.act3ai.com/password-reset) or contact support.
- **Need to upgrade?** → Go to [Subscription Plan](/docs/legacy-wiki/subscription-plan "Subscription Plan") for options.
