## Overview

Lighting in [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI") defines how scenes, shots, and characters are illuminated to achieve mood, realism, or stylization. Alongside [Camera\_Angles](/docs/legacy-wiki/camera-angles "Camera Angles") and [Cinematography](/docs/legacy-wiki/cinematography "Cinematography"), lighting helps convey emotion, highlight characters, and direct audience focus. Users can customize light sources, intensity, and dynamics through the platform’s visual pre-production tools.

## 1. Purpose

- Establish the mood and atmosphere of each [Scenes](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes")
- Emphasize characters, props, and environments
- Simulate realistic time-of-day conditions (sunrise, sunset, night, etc.)
- Guide viewer attention through contrast and highlights

## 2. Lighting Types

- **Key Light:** Main source that defines subject shape
- **Fill Light:** Softens shadows, balances exposure
- **Back Light (Rim):** Separates subject from background
- **Practical Lights:** Lamps, candles, neon signs visible in shot
- **Environmental Light:** Sky, moonlight, ambient bounce

## 3. System Features

- AI-assisted auto-lighting per [Shots](/docs/legacy-wiki/shots "Shots") or [Scenes](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes")
- Adjustable presets: cinematic, natural, dramatic, stylized
- Dynamic lighting for [animated](/docs/legacy-wiki/animation-studios "Animation Studios") or VFX-heavy projects
- Time-of-day simulation tied to [Story\_Beats](/docs/legacy-wiki/story-beats "Story Beats")
- Light rig visualization in [Top\_Down\_View](/docs/legacy-wiki/top-down-view "Top Down View")

## 4. Lighting Controls

- Intensity (lumens / exposure value)
- Color temperature (Kelvin scale, e.g. warm 3200K vs cool 5600K)
- Direction (front, side, top, back)
- Shadows (soft vs hard, cast length)
- Flicker and movement for practical lights (fire, neon, torch)

## 5. Integration with Tools

- Works with [Storyboard\_Panels](/docs/legacy-wiki/storyboard-panels "Storyboard Panels") to pre-visualize scenes
- Synchronized with [Camera Angles](/docs/legacy-wiki/camera-angles "Camera Angles") and shot composition
- Auto-adjusts for [Digital\_Actors](/docs/legacy-wiki/digital-actors "Digital Actors") skin tones and costume materials
- Supports AI render models ([Google\_Veo\_3](/docs/legacy-wiki/google-veo-3 "Google Veo 3"), [Runway](/docs/legacy-wiki/runway "Runway"), [Wan](/docs/legacy-wiki/wan "Wan"))

## 6. Example Workflows

1. Select a [Scenes](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes") and enable "Lighting Setup" mode
2. Choose a preset (e.g., "Film Noir") or create a custom rig
3. Adjust exposure and color balance per [Shots](/docs/legacy-wiki/shots "Shots")
4. Preview results in [Editor](/docs/legacy-wiki/editor "Editor") or [Build\_Video](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video")
5. Refine during [render pass](/docs/legacy-wiki/rendering-system "Rendering System") for final look

## 7. Roadmap

- Volumetric lighting support (fog, god rays)
- Automated “lighting continuity” between scenes
- HDR and Dolby Vision grading integration
- Community lighting presets exchange
