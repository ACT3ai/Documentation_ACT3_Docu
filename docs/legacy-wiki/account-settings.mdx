# Account Settings

## Overview

The **Account Settings** section in [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI") allows you to manage your personal profile, security, billing, and organization preferences.
It is accessible from the top-right user menu in the [Dashboard](/docs/legacy-wiki/dashboard "Dashboard") or directly from the sidebar under [Organization Management](/docs/legacy-wiki/organization-management "Organization Management").

## Key Features

- **Profile Management:** Update your name, email, and profile picture.
- **Password & Security:** Change password, enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
- **Billing Settings:** Manage payment methods, view invoices, update [subscription plans](/docs/legacy-wiki/subscription-plan "Subscription Plan").
- **Credit Monitoring:** Check [credit balance](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System") and purchase additional credits.
- **Organization Settings:** If you’re part of a team, manage roles, permissions, and shared credit pools.
- **Notifications:** Customize email and in-app alerts.

## How to Access

1. Log in at [https://app.act3ai.com](https://app.act3ai.com).
2. Click your profile icon (top-right corner).
3. Select **Account Settings** from the dropdown.
4. Navigate tabs: Profile · Security · Billing · Organization · Notifications.

## Sections in Account Settings

### Profile

- Name, display name, and profile image.
- Contact email and linked accounts (Google, Apple, etc.).

### Security

- Update password.
- Enable 2FA for extra protection.
- Manage active sessions and log out of devices.

### Billing

- Update payment methods (credit card, PayPal).
- View invoices and billing history.
- Upgrade or downgrade your [Subscription Plan](/docs/legacy-wiki/subscription-plan "Subscription Plan").
- Apply promotional codes.

### Credits

- View current [credit balance](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System").
- Purchase extra credits.
- Track project-level credit usage.

### Organization

- Add or remove members.
- Assign roles (Owner, Admin, Editor, Viewer).
- Manage shared credit pools.
- Configure permissions for project editing.

### Notifications

- Toggle email alerts for billing, renders, and collaboration.
- Set in-app notification preferences.

## Best Practices

- Enable 2FA to protect your account.
- Regularly update your password.
- Monitor [credit usage](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System") to avoid workflow interruptions.
- Assign clear roles in [Organization Management](/docs/legacy-wiki/organization-management "Organization Management").
- Review invoices monthly in [Billing History](/docs/legacy-wiki/billing-model "Billing Model").

## Troubleshooting

- **Can’t log in** → Reset password or use 2FA backup codes.
- **Billing error** → Check payment method or contact support.
- **Missing credits** → Refresh balance or verify in [Credit Dashboard](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System").
- **Organization settings locked** → Confirm you have admin or owner role.
