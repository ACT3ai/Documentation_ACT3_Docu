# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is ACT 3 AI?

ACT 3 AI is an AI-powered video production platform that helps creators transform ideas or scripts into [production-ready videos](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video").
It combines [AI story generation](/docs/legacy-wiki/ai-wizard "AI Wizard"), [Digital Actors](/docs/legacy-wiki/digital-actors "Digital Actors"), and advanced [editing tools](/docs/legacy-wiki/editor "Editor") in a single workspace.

### Who can use ACT 3 AI?

Anyone—from [filmmakers and marketers](/docs/legacy-wiki/target-customers "Target Customers") to students and social media creators—can use the platform.
You don’t need technical skills, but knowledge of [scriptwriting](/docs/legacy-wiki/script-editing "Script Editing") and [storyboarding](https://wiki.act3ai.com/index.php?title=Storyboarding&action=edit&redlink=1 "Storyboarding (page does not exist)") can help.

### Is there a free trial?

Yes, ACT 3 AI offers limited credits for new accounts. See [Billing Model](/docs/legacy-wiki/billing-model "Billing Model") and [Credit System](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System") for details.

## Technical Questions

### Which file formats are supported?

See [File Formats](/docs/legacy-wiki/file-formats "File Formats") for a complete list of supported video, audio, image, and 3D asset formats.

### Can I import my own script?

Yes, you can use [Import Script](/docs/legacy-wiki/import-script "Import Script") to bring in FDX, TXT, or Markdown scripts. You can also paste text directly into the [script editor](/docs/legacy-wiki/editor "Editor").

### Can I export my video in 4K?

Yes, ACT 3 AI supports 4K exports in MP4 and ProRes formats. See [Build Video](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video") for settings.

### Which AI models can I use for video generation?

ACT 3 AI integrates with:

- [Google Veo 3](/docs/legacy-wiki/google-veo-3 "Google Veo 3")
- [WAN AI Integration](/docs/legacy-wiki/wan "Wan")
- [Runway Integration](/docs/legacy-wiki/runway "Runway")

Each has different strengths and [credit costs](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System").

## Collaboration & Accounts

### Can multiple people work on the same project?

Yes. [Collaboration features](/docs/legacy-wiki/collaboration "Collaboration") let multiple users edit scripts, [scenes](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes"), and [shots](/docs/legacy-wiki/shots "Shots") in real time.

### How do I invite team members?

You can invite users from the [Organization settings](/docs/legacy-wiki/organization "Organization") panel.

### Can I lock parts of my project?

Yes, you can lock [scenes](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes") or [shots](/docs/legacy-wiki/shots "Shots") to prevent changes after approval.

## Troubleshooting

### My renders are taking too long. What should I do?

Try lowering preview quality in the [Editor preview settings](/docs/legacy-wiki/editor "Editor") or using [WAN AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/wan "Wan") for quick iterations.

### My AI-generated actors look wrong.

Adjust their parameters in [Digital Actors](/docs/legacy-wiki/digital-actors "Digital Actors") or [Characters](/docs/legacy-wiki/characters "Characters") to refine their appearance.

### I’m getting a “Not Enough Credits” error.

Check your [credit balance](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System") and consider upgrading via [Subscription Plan](/docs/legacy-wiki/subscription-plan "Subscription Plan").
