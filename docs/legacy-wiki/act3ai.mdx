## Overview

**ACT 3 AI** is a next-generation AI filmmaking platform that empowers creators to transform ideas and scripts into production-ready videos.
It combines AI writing, directing, casting, cinematography, and rendering into one workflow, enabling both solo creators and professional studios to create high-quality content with speed and efficiency.

*“Write less, direct more.”* — ACT 3 AI Mission

---

## What We Do

ACT 3 AI provides:

- End-to-end video creation, from [new projects](/docs/legacy-wiki/new-project "New Project") to [final renders](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video")
- Story development with [AI Wizard](/docs/legacy-wiki/ai-wizard "AI Wizard")
- Script handling via [Script Editor](/docs/legacy-wiki/script-editor "Script Editor"), [Import Script](/docs/legacy-wiki/import-script "Import Script"), and [Export Script](/docs/legacy-wiki/export-script "Export Script")
- [Character creation](/docs/legacy-wiki/characters "Characters") and [Digital Actors](/docs/legacy-wiki/digital-actors "Digital Actors") for AI-powered performances
- [Central Editor](/docs/legacy-wiki/editor "Editor") with [Timeline](/docs/legacy-wiki/timeline "Timeline"), [Storyboards](/docs/legacy-wiki/storyboard-panels "Storyboard Panels"), and [Top-Down Planning](/docs/legacy-wiki/top-down-view "Top Down View")
- Integration with leading AI video engines: [Google Veo 3](/docs/legacy-wiki/google-veo-3 "Google Veo 3"), [Runway](/docs/legacy-wiki/runway "Runway"), and [WAN AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/wan "Wan")

## Workflow

| Step | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Idea | Start with a simple description or concept. |
| [AI Wizard](/docs/legacy-wiki/ai-wizard "AI Wizard") | Expand into story arcs, beats, and acts. |
| [Script Editor](/docs/legacy-wiki/script-editor "Script Editor") | Write or import a script. |
| [Characters & Digital Actors](/docs/legacy-wiki/characters "Characters") | Cast and customize performances. |
| [Editor Workspace](/docs/legacy-wiki/editor "Editor") | Plan visually with timeline, storyboards, and top-down view. |
| [Google Veo 3](/docs/legacy-wiki/google-veo-3 "Google Veo 3"), [Runway](/docs/legacy-wiki/runway "Runway"), [WAN AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/wan "Wan") | Generate AI video clips. |
| [Build Video](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video") & [Render Queue](/docs/legacy-wiki/render-queue "Render Queue") | Assemble, refine, and export final content. |
