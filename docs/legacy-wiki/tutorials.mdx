# Overview

The **Tutorials** section provides step-by-step guides, tips, and best practices for getting the most out of [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI").
These tutorials cover the complete workflow—from [starting a project](/docs/legacy-wiki/getting-started "Getting Started") to [exporting your final video](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video")—and include advanced creative techniques.

## Beginner Tutorials

### 1. Create Your First Project

- Learn to start a [New Project](/docs/legacy-wiki/new-project "New Project"), set project details, and choose visual styles.
- Covers basic [script editing](/docs/legacy-wiki/script-editing "Script Editing"), [scene creation](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes"), and [preview rendering](/docs/legacy-wiki/render-queue "Render Queue").
- See: [Getting Started Guide](/docs/legacy-wiki/getting-started "Getting Started")

### 2. Using the AI Wizard

- Walkthrough on [AI Wizard](/docs/legacy-wiki/ai-wizard "AI Wizard") for generating story arcs, acts, and beats.
- Tips for refining [story beats](/docs/legacy-wiki/story-beats "Story Beats") and integrating them into your [scenes](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes").

### 3. Importing & Editing Scripts

- Learn how to [import scripts](/docs/legacy-wiki/import-script "Import Script") from Final Draft or plain text.
- Use the [Script editor](/docs/legacy-wiki/editor "Editor") to format, edit, and structure your screenplay.

## Intermediate Tutorials

### 4. Working with Digital Actors

- Create and customize [Digital Actors](/docs/legacy-wiki/digital-actors "Digital Actors") and [characters](/docs/legacy-wiki/characters "Characters").
- Assign them to [scenes](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes") and [shots](/docs/legacy-wiki/shots "Shots") for visual continuity.

### 5. Storyboarding & Camera Planning

- Use [Storyboard Panels](/docs/legacy-wiki/storyboard-panels "Storyboard Panels") and [Top-Down View](/docs/legacy-wiki/top-down-view "Top Down View") to plan your shots.
- Explore [camera angles](/docs/legacy-wiki/camera-angles "Camera Angles") and shot composition.

### 6. Importing B-Roll

- Add additional footage using [Import B-Roll](/docs/legacy-wiki/import-broll "Import BRoll").
- Tips for blending B-Roll with AI-generated scenes.

## Advanced Tutorials

### 7. Advanced Rendering Options

- Learn to use [Google Veo 3](/docs/legacy-wiki/google-veo-3 "Google Veo 3"), [WAN AI Integration](/docs/legacy-wiki/wan "Wan"), and [Runway Integration](/docs/legacy-wiki/runway "Runway").
- Adjust [credit usage](/docs/legacy-wiki/credit-system "Credit System") for optimal quality and cost.

### 8. Building & Exporting Videos

- Use [Build Video](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video") to compile final edits.
- Export in different [file formats](/docs/legacy-wiki/file-formats "File Formats") and resolutions.

### 9. Collaboration Features

- Work with your [organization](/docs/legacy-wiki/organization "Organization") or team.
- Lock [scenes](/docs/legacy-wiki/scenes "Scenes") and [shots](/docs/legacy-wiki/shots "Shots"), and leave comments for feedback.

## Tips & Tricks

- Use detailed prompts for better AI output.
- Keep shot descriptions under 100 words for faster rendering.
- Combine [Digital Actors](/docs/legacy-wiki/digital-actors "Digital Actors") with custom props and lighting for unique visuals.
