## Overview

The **Credit System** in [ACT 3 AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/act3ai "ACT3AI") is a flexible virtual currency model that allows you to pay only for the AI features, render durations, and output qualities you use.
Credits are deducted in real time whenever you generate previews, perform full renders, or use premium AI tools such as [Google Veo 3](/docs/legacy-wiki/google-veo-3 "Google Veo 3"), [WAN AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/wan "Wan"), or [Runway Integration](/docs/legacy-wiki/runway "Runway").

Credits can be part of a monthly [subscription plan](/docs/legacy-wiki/subscription-plan "Subscription Plan") or purchased on demand through pay-as-you-go top-ups.

## Key Features

- **Universal Usage**: Credits work across all rendering engines and integrations.
- **Real-Time Tracking**: See your credit balance at the top of the [dashboard](/docs/legacy-wiki/dashboard "Dashboard") and in the Credits Panel.
- **Flexible Spending**: Choose quality levels and rendering engines to control credit consumption.
- **Team Sharing**: Pool credits across an [organization](/docs/legacy-wiki/organization "Organization") for collaborative projects.

## How Credits Are Used

Credits are consumed when you:

- Generate AI previews in the [Editor workspace](/docs/legacy-wiki/editor "Editor")
- Render final-quality videos via [Build Video](/docs/legacy-wiki/build-video "Build Video")
- Run [Google Veo 3](/docs/legacy-wiki/google-veo-3 "Google Veo 3") or [Runway Integration](/docs/legacy-wiki/runway "Runway") tasks
- Host real-time [WAN AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/wan "Wan") collaboration sessions
- Export advanced outputs (4K video, multi-language dubs, extended takes)

## Credit Consumption Factors

Credit usage varies based on:

- **Resolution** (Draft, Medium, High, 4K)
- **Render Length** (per second or per minute)
- **Engine Type** ([Google Veo 3](/docs/legacy-wiki/google-veo-3 "Google Veo 3"), [WAN AI](/docs/legacy-wiki/wan "Wan"), [Runway](/docs/legacy-wiki/runway "Runway"))
- **Feature Add-ons** (e.g., advanced color grading, multi-language dubs)

## Example Credit Usage

| Feature | Draft (1080p) | High (1080p) | 4K |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Preview Render (per 10s) | 1 credit | 2 credits | 4 credits |
| Full Render (per 10s) | 3 credits | 5 credits | 10 credits |
| [Google Veo 3](/docs/legacy-wiki/google-veo-3 "Google Veo 3") (per 10s) | 4 credits | 6 credits | 12 credits |
| [WAN AI Session](/docs/legacy-wiki/wan "Wan") (per min) | 2 credits | 3 credits | N/A |

## Managing Your Credits

- Use draft previews for testing instead of high-quality renders.
- Plan scenes in the [Storyboard & Panels](/docs/legacy-wiki/storyboard-panels "Storyboard Panels") before generating.
- Lock completed shots to prevent accidental re-renders.
- Schedule rendering batches overnight in the [Render Queue](/docs/legacy-wiki/render-queue "Render Queue").

## Buying More Credits

- Purchase credit packs anytime from the Credit Store
- Upgrade your [subscription plan](/docs/legacy-wiki/subscription-plan "Subscription Plan") for a higher monthly credit allowance
- Enterprise plans can negotiate custom credit rates and annual bundles
