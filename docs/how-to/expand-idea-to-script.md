# How to Expand an Idea into a Script

**Goal:** Turn a rough story idea — anything from a sentence to a few paragraphs — into a structured screenplay with acts, beats, scenes, and dialogue, ready to send to shot generation.

You don't need a finished script to use ACT3 AI. The story expansion system builds the full structure for you, one level at a time, with your approval at each step.

---

## Steps

1. **Start a new project.** From the Projects dashboard, click **+ New Project**. When asked how you want to start, choose **Expand from Idea**.

2. **Open the AI Wizard.** The wizard launches. It guides you through the setup — project type (Movie, TV Episode, Short, Explainer), target duration, and visual style.

3. **Enter your idea.** In the text box, describe your story. This can be:
   - A one-line logline: *"A retired detective is pulled back in when her sister goes missing."*
   - A paragraph of rough plot
   - Pasted text from a document, article, or even ChatGPT output
   - A Wikipedia page or book summary for an adaptation

4. **Review and approve the logline and theme.** The AI proposes a refined logline, a central theme, and a core conflict. Accept them, edit them, or rewrite them entirely before moving on.

5. **Review and approve the act structure.** The AI breaks the story into acts and major beats. Reorder beats by dragging, delete ones that don't fit, or add new ones. Click **Accept Acts** when ready.

6. **Review and develop characters.** The AI proposes your main cast with summaries and relationships. Add, remove, or edit characters. These will become your digital actors later.

7. **Approve scene generation.** With the act and beat structure locked, click **Generate Scenes**. The AI builds the full scene list. Review and adjust.

8. **Generate the full script.** Click **Generate Script**. The AI writes action lines and dialogue for every scene. Your script is now in the editor and your shot list is ready to develop.
