# Marketing Videos

ACT3 AI is a fast, cost-effective way for marketing teams and agencies to produce high-quality video content — without renting a studio, hiring a crew, or waiting weeks for post-production.

## Who this section is for

- In-house brand marketing teams who need video fast
- Marketing agencies producing content for multiple clients
- Anyone replacing expensive production shoots with AI-generated video

## What you can do

- **Script to screen in hours, not weeks** — Paste in your copy, let the AI structure it into [scenes](/concepts/scenes) and [shots](/concepts/shots), then render.
- **Brand-consistent visuals** — Control color palette, [visual style](/how-to/set-visual-style), and set design to match your brand.
- **Multiple formats from one project** — [Export](/how-to/export-for-delivery) 16:9 for YouTube, 9:16 vertical for TikTok and Reels, and 1:1 for Instagram from the same production.
- **Rapid iteration** — Change a line of copy or a visual style and [regenerate](/how-to/regenerate-a-shot) only the affected shots.
- **Reusable templates** — Save scene packs and sets for recurring content series.
