# Explainer Videos

**Who this section is for:** Marketers, educators, product teams, and content creators who need to explain something complex in a way that's clear, engaging, and easy to understand.

An explainer video has one job: take something the viewer doesn't understand and make them understand it. Whether you're explaining a SaaS product, a medical procedure, a financial concept, or how a piece of technology works — the craft is the same. You need a clear narrative arc, visual support for every abstract idea, and a pace that gives the viewer time to follow along.

ACT3 AI is particularly well-suited to explainers because the platform handles visual storytelling structure natively — you don't need to think like an animator or video producer to produce a great one.

---

## What you can make here

- **Product explainers** — How your software or service works
- **Process explainers** — How a complex process is done step by step
- **Concept explainers** — Making an abstract idea visual and understandable
- **Educational videos** — Teaching a skill, a subject, or a framework
- **Onboarding videos** — Walk new users or customers through getting started
