# Screenwriters

If you have a script — or even just a story idea — ACT3 AI turns it into a fully rendered AI video production.

You don't need to know 3D software, video editing tools, or AI prompting techniques. Write the story. ACT3 AI handles the rest.

## What you can do here

- **Import your script** — Paste in a Final Draft export, a PDF, or plain text. ACT3 AI parses your scene headings, action lines, and dialogue automatically.
- **Start from an idea** — No finished script? Describe your story in a sentence or a paragraph. The AI expands it into a full screenplay with acts, scenes, and shots.
- **Visualize scenes instantly** — Every scene becomes a shot list. Every shot becomes an AI-generated video clip.
- **Stay in creative control** — Review, approve, or rewrite anything the AI proposes before rendering. You are the director.
