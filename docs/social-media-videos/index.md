# Social Media Videos

ACT3 AI is built for high-output social media creators. Go from idea to exported video fast — in the right format for every platform.

## Who this section is for

- YouTube creators who want cinematic-looking content without a film crew
- TikTok and Reels creators who need to publish frequently
- Anyone building a video content channel powered by AI

## What you can do

- **Idea to episode overnight** — Describe your concept, let the AI write the script, generate the shots, and export by morning.
- **All social formats from one project** — 16:9 for YouTube, vertical 9:16 for TikTok and Reels, 1:1 for Instagram.
- **Multiple visual styles** — Cinematic Realism, 3D Animated, Cartoon 2D, or Anime — pick the look that matches your channel.
- **AI-generated thumbnails** — Algorithm-friendly thumbnail variations from your rendered frames.
- **Fast iteration** — Don't like a shot? Regenerate it in minutes without starting over.

## Guides in this section

Platform-specific export settings, format recommendations, and workflows for high-frequency social media video production.
