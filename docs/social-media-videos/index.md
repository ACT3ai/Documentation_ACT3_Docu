# Social Media Videos

ACT3 AI is built for high-output social media creators. Go from idea to exported video in the right format for every platform — fast enough to maintain a consistent publishing schedule.

---

## Who this section is for

- YouTube creators who want cinematic-looking content without a film crew
- TikTok and Reels creators who need to publish frequently and consistently
- Brand content teams producing short-form video at scale
- Anyone building a video content channel powered by AI

---

## What you can do

- **Idea to episode fast** — Describe your concept, let the AI write the [script](/how-to/use-the-script-editor), generate the [shots](/how-to/set-up-a-shot), and export in the right format.
- **All social formats from one project** — 16:9 for YouTube, vertical 9:16 for TikTok and Reels, 1:1 for Instagram.
- **Multiple visual styles** — [Cinematic Realism, 3D Animated, Cartoon 2D, or Anime](/how-to/set-visual-style) — pick the look that matches your channel.
- **Consistent recurring characters** — Build your host or cast as [digital actors](/how-to/create-digital-actor) and reuse them in every video. Consistent talent builds audience recognition faster than any other factor.
- **Fast iteration** — Don't like a shot? [Regenerate it](/how-to/regenerate-a-shot) in minutes without starting over.
- **High-volume production** — Use the [render queue](/how-to/manage-render-queue) to batch-render multiple videos in one run.

## Key concepts for social media video

Understanding these ACT3 AI features will help you produce social video faster:

- **[Visual Style](/how-to/set-visual-style)** — Set once at the project level. All videos in the series inherit it.
- **[Digital Actors](/how-to/create-digital-actor)** — Create your recurring host or cast once. Reuse them across every video.
- **[Shot Setup](/how-to/set-up-a-shot)** — 22 shot types including medium, close-up, and POV. For vertical video, use medium shots and close-ups centered for the tall frame.
- **[Voice Assignment](/how-to/assign-character-voice)** — Assign a consistent TTS voice to your on-screen character. The same voice across all videos strengthens channel identity.
- **[Export for Platforms](/how-to/export-for-platforms)** — One project, multiple format exports: 9:16, 16:9, 1:1.
- **[Render Queue](/how-to/manage-render-queue)** — Batch-render multiple videos simultaneously. Submit everything, come back when it's done.
