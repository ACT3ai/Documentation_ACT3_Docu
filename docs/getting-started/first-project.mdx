# Your First Project

This guide walks you through creating a project and generating your first video clip.

## Steps

1. Log in and click **New Project**
2. Paste or type your script
3. Use the Beat editor to mark story beats
4. Expand a beat into scenes and shots
5. Click **Generate** on a shot to produce video
6. Review and export
