# Dashboard

The Dashboard is your home base in ACT3 AI. It gives you an at-a-glance view of your active projects, credit balance, render jobs, and storage — and provides quick access to the most-used tools.

## What You See on the Dashboard

**Project Overview** — A list of your active projects with their current status: Drafting, Rendering, or Completed. Click any project to open it in the editor.

**Credit and Plan Summary** — Shows your current plan tier, credits available this month, rollover bank balance, and a usage forecast based on recent activity. A warning appears when your balance drops below 20% and again at 5%.

**Storage Meter** — Visual indicator of how much cloud storage you have used versus your plan limit, broken down by project or organization.

**Job Activity Feed** — Live updates from the render queue, showing which jobs are queued, rendering, completed, or failed.

**Quick Actions** — One-click shortcuts to start a new project, open the script editor, or launch the AI Wizard.

## Navigating to Key Tools

From the dashboard sidebar you can reach:

- **New Project** — Start a fresh production
- **AI Wizard** — Generate a story outline from a concept
- **[Script Editor](/features/script-editing)** — Write or edit a script
- **Characters / [Digital Actors](/features/actors)** — Manage your actor library
- **Top-Down View** — Visual camera planning tool (see [Top-Down Editor](/how-to/top-down-set-editor))
- **Build Video / [Render Queue](/features/render-queue)** — Video assembly and rendering
- **Cloud Storage** — Browse and manage uploaded assets
- **Settings** — Account, billing, and organization management (see [Organization Settings](/account/settings-organization))

## Role-Based Views

What you see on the dashboard depends on your role:

- **Writers** — See scripts, [beats](/concepts/beats), and job previews
- **Directors** — Access camera controls, Top-Down View, and render jobs
- **Admins** — Full access including billing, credit usage, and organization settings
- **Viewers** — Read-only access to project summaries and completed renders

## Alerts and Notifications

The dashboard surfaces important alerts automatically:

- Low credit balance warnings (20% and 5%)
- Cloud storage alerts (80% and 95% full)
- Failed render jobs with error details
- Invitation notifications when teammates join your organization

## Tips

- Pin frequently used projects to the top of the project list for fast access
- Check the job activity feed before starting new renders to avoid queue conflicts
- Use the Quick Actions bar to jump straight to the AI Wizard for a new project concept
- Admins can view per-user and per-project credit consumption in the organization settings panel
