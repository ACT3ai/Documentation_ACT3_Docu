# Introduction to ACT3 AI

ACT3 AI is an end-to-end AI filmmaking platform that transforms ideas and scripts into production-ready video. It unifies writing, directing, casting, cinematography, and rendering into one seamless workflow so you can focus on storytelling while AI handles the technical overhead.

*"Write less, direct more."*

## What ACT3 AI Does

ACT3 AI connects every stage of production in a single workspace:

- **Script to screen** — Write or import a script, then watch it become structured scenes, shots, and rendered video
- **AI video generation** — Generate clips using Google Veo 3.1, Grok Video, or Wan 2.1
- **Digital actors** — Cast AI-generated characters with customizable appearance, voice, and motion capture
- **Cinematography control** — Direct camera angles, movement, lighting, and set design with cinematic precision
- **Built-in video editor** — Assemble and export your finished production in the same platform

## The Core Workflow

Every ACT3 AI project follows the same path:

```
Script → Beats → Acts → Scenes → Shots → AI Video Generation → Edit → Export
```

| Step | What Happens |
|---|---|
| **Script** | Write dialogue and action, or import an existing screenplay |
| **Beats** | Break the story into key narrative moments that drive pacing |
| **Acts** | Divide the story into major dramatic segments |
| **Scenes** | Group shots by location and time |
| **Shots** | Define individual camera setups with cinematic instructions |
| **AI Video Generation** | Render shots using your chosen AI engine |
| **Edit** | Assemble clips on the timeline, add audio, apply color grading |
| **Export** | Deliver in MP4, ProRes, 4K, or platform-optimized formats |

## Why ACT3 AI

Traditional video production requires weeks of pre-production, expensive crews, and physical sets. ACT3 AI reduces those barriers:

- Reduce 80–200 hours of pre-production to a few hours
- Eliminate location, crew, and equipment costs for many project types
- Enable solo creators and small teams to achieve studio-quality workflows
- Preserve full creative control with human-in-the-loop editing at every stage

## Platform Access

ACT3 AI is a web application. You access it in any modern browser — Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge — on Mac, Windows, or Linux. No local software installation is required.

The optional Hero Blender Sync desktop app (Mac and Windows) is available for projects that use Blender integration. Everything else runs in the browser.

## Scale: From Short Films to Feature Length

ACT3 AI handles projects at any scale:

- Social media clips (15–90 seconds)
- Short films (2–20 minutes)
- Feature films (up to 2+ hours)
- TV series with full season and episode hierarchies

A 40-minute film typically requires approximately 650 shots. The render queue handles batches of shots in parallel, and the platform's tagging, locking, and outline tools keep large productions organized.
