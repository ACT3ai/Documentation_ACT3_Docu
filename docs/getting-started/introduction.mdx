# Introduction to ACT3 AI

ACT3 AI is an end-to-end AI filmmaking platform that transforms ideas and scripts into production-ready video. It unifies writing, directing, casting, cinematography, and rendering into one seamless workflow so you can focus on storytelling while AI handles the technical overhead.

*"Write less, direct more."*

## What ACT3 AI Does

ACT3 AI connects every stage of production in a single workspace:

- **[Script](/features/script-editing) to screen** — Write or import a script, then watch it become structured scenes, shots, and rendered video
- **[AI video generation](/features/ai-video-generation)** — Generate clips using Google Veo 3.1, Grok Video, or Wan 2.1
- **[Digital actors](/features/actors)** — Cast AI-generated characters with customizable appearance, voice, and motion capture
- **[Cinematography](/features/cinematography) control** — Direct camera angles, movement, lighting, and set design with cinematic precision
- **Built-in [video editor](/features/video-editor)** — Assemble and export your finished production in the same platform

## The Core Workflow

Every ACT3 AI project follows the same path:

```
Script → Beats → Acts → Scenes → Shots → AI Video Generation → Edit → Export
```

| Step | What Happens |
|---|---|
| **[Script](/features/script-editing)** | Write dialogue and action, or import an existing screenplay |
| **[Beats](/concepts/beats)** | Break the story into key narrative moments that drive pacing |
| **[Acts](/concepts/acts)** | Divide the story into major dramatic segments |
| **[Scenes](/concepts/scenes)** | Group shots by location and time |
| **[Shots](/concepts/shots)** | Define individual camera setups with cinematic instructions |
| **[AI Video Generation](/features/ai-video-generation)** | Render shots using your chosen AI engine |
| **[Edit](/features/video-editor)** | Assemble clips on the timeline, add audio, apply color grading |
| **[Export](/export/overview)** | Deliver in MP4, ProRes, 4K, or platform-optimized formats |

## Why ACT3 AI

Traditional video production requires weeks of pre-production, expensive crews, and physical sets. ACT3 AI reduces those barriers:

- Reduce 80–200 hours of pre-production to a few hours
- Eliminate location, crew, and equipment costs for many project types
- Enable solo creators and small teams to achieve studio-quality workflows
- Preserve full creative control with human-in-the-loop editing at every stage

## Platform Access

ACT3 AI is a web application. You access it in any modern browser — Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge — on Mac, Windows, or Linux. No local software installation is required.

The optional Hero Blender Sync desktop app (Mac and Windows) is available for projects that use [Blender](/integrations/blender) integration. Everything else runs in the browser.

## Scale: From Short Films to Feature Length

ACT3 AI handles projects at any scale:

- Social media clips (15–90 seconds)
- Short films (2–20 minutes)
- Feature films (up to 2+ hours)
- TV series with full season and episode hierarchies

A 40-minute film typically requires approximately 650 shots. The [render queue](/features/render-queue) handles batches of shots in parallel, and the platform's tagging, locking, and outline tools keep large productions organized.

## The Editor — Your Primary Workspace

Everything in ACT3 AI comes together in the Editor. It is where you write and revise your script, arrange shots on the timeline, preview AI-generated clips, block scenes in the Top-Down View, and send your final cut to render. If you learn one thing first, learn the Editor.

**[→ Open the Video Editor Guide](/features/video-editor)**

The Editor has dedicated sections for every stage of production:

- **[Script Pane](/features/video-editor)** — Write, import, and revise your screenplay alongside your timeline
- **[Timeline](/features/video-editor)** — Arrange scenes, shots, B-roll, and audio tracks with drag-and-drop precision
- **[Preview Pane](/features/video-editor)** — Play draft renders and scrub to any frame without leaving the Editor
- **[Top-Down View](/features/video-editor)** — Block your scene with a bird's-eye canvas — place actors, cameras, and set movement paths
- **[Color Grading](/features/video-editor)** — Apply LUTs and manual color adjustments for a unified visual look
- **[Collaboration Tools](/features/collaboration)** — See who is editing, leave comments, and lock approved sections
