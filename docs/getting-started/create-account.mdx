# Create Your Account

Creating an ACT3 AI account gives you access to the full platform including project creation, the AI Wizard, digital actors, and video rendering. Accounts are free to create and can be upgraded at any time.

## Sign Up

1. Go to [app.act3ai.com](https://app.act3ai.com)
2. Click **Sign Up** on the login page
3. Enter your full name, email address, and a password (minimum 8 characters)
4. Check your inbox and click the verification link
5. Log in with your new credentials

You will land on your dashboard, where you can start your first project immediately.

## Personal vs Organization Accounts

**Personal Account** — For individual creators working solo. All projects, assets, and credits belong to you.

**Organization Account** — For teams who need shared projects, pooled credits, and role-based permissions. One owner controls billing; members are invited by email and assigned roles (Admin, Editor, Viewer).

You can start with a personal account and create or join an organization later from your account settings.

## What You Get on Sign-Up

Every new account starts with:

- **800 free credits** to generate previews and explore features
- Access to all core tools: script editor, AI Wizard, digital actors, editor, and timeline
- 50 MB of cloud storage

The free tier is available for your first 6 months. After that, a paid plan is required to continue generating content.

## Account Management

Once logged in, your account settings are accessible from the profile icon in the top-right corner. From there you can:

- Update your name, email, and profile picture
- Change your password or enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
- View and manage your subscription plan
- Check your credit balance and purchase additional credits
- Manage team members if you run an organization

## Troubleshooting

**Didn't receive the confirmation email?** Check your spam or junk folder, then use the resend option on the login page.

**Forgot your password?** Use the reset form at [app.act3ai.com/password-reset](https://app.act3ai.com/password-reset).

**Need to upgrade?** Go to Settings → Plans & Billing, or see the [Billing](/docs/account/billing) page for a full plan comparison.
